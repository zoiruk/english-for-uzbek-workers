Grammar / Grammatika

Grammatika - Present Simple harakat fe'llari bilan (Present Simple with everyday verbs)

Bu grammatika nima uchun kerak? (Why do we need this grammar?)

Present Simple zamonini harakat fe'llari bilan ishlatib, siz har kuni qiladigan ishlar haqida gapirasiz. Mavsumiy ishchi sifatida bu zamonni ishda, uyda va kundalik hayotda doimiy ishlatasiz - ish vazifalarini tushuntirish, kun tartibini aytish va odatlaringiz haqida gapirish uchun.

Misol: "Men har kuni qulupnay teraman" - "I pick strawberries every day"

Asosiy tushuncha (Basic Concept)

Harakat fe'llari bilan Present Simple ishlatganda, biz har kuni takrorlanadigan ishlar va odatlar haqida gapiramiz. Muhim qoidalar:

  • I, You, We, They bilan fe'lning asosiy shaklini ishlatamiz: work, pick, carry
  • He, She, It bilan fe'lga -s yoki -es qo'shamiz: works, picks, carries
  • Inkor: do not (don't) / does not (doesn't) + asosiy fe'l
  • Savol: Do/Does + subject + asosiy fe'l

Uch shakl (Three Forms)

1. Tasdiqlovchi shakl (Positive Form)

Qachon ishlatamiz: Har kuni qiladigan ishlar va odatlar haqida gapirishda

Subject Verb Form Example O'zbek ma'nosi
I/You/We/They pick I pick strawberries Men qulupnay teraman
He/She/It picks She picks strawberries U qulupnay teradi
Amaliy misollar (Practical Examples):
I wake up at 6 AM every day.
[Ай вейк ап эт сикс эй эм эври дей]
Men har kuni ertalab 6 da uyg'onaman.
Kun tartibi: Ertalabki odatlar
We carry heavy boxes to the truck.
[Ви кэри хэви боксиз ту зэ трак]
Biz og'ir qutilarni yuk mashinasiga ko'taramiz.
Ish vazifasi: Jismoniy ish
She plants vegetables in the greenhouse.
[Ши плэнтс веджетэблз ин зэ гринхаус]
U issiqxonada sabzavot ekadi.
Ferma ishi: Ekish jarayoni
They help each other with difficult tasks.
[Зей хэлп ич азэр виз дификалт таскс]
Ular bir-birlariga qiyin ishlarda yordam berishadi.
Jamoaviy ish: Hamkorlik
He opens the farm gate at 7 AM.
[Хи оупэнз зэ фарм гейт эт севен эй эм]
U ertalab 7 da ferma darvozasini ochadi.
Ish boshlanishi: Kun tartibi

2. Inkorlovchi shakl (Negative Form)

Qachon ishlatamiz: Qilmaydigan ishlar yoki yo'q odatlar haqida gapirishda

Qanday yasaymiz: do not (don't) / does not (doesn't) + asosiy fe'l

Subject Do/Does + Not Verb O'zbek ma'nosi
I/You/We/They don't work ishlamayman/ishlamaysiz
He/She/It doesn't work ishlamaydi
Amaliy misollar (Practical Examples):
I don't work on Sundays.
[Ай донт ворк он Сандейз]
Men yakshanba kunlari ishlamayman.
Dam olish: Haftalik jadval
We don't pick fruit in the rain.
[Ви донт пик фрут ин зэ рейн]
Biz yomg'irda meva termaymiz.
Ish qoidalari: Ob-havo sharoiti
She doesn't carry heavy boxes alone.
[Ши дазнт кэри хэви боксиз элоун]
U og'ir qutilarni yolg'iz ko'tarmaydi.
Xavfsizlik: Ish qoidalari
They don't eat lunch before 12 PM.
[Зей донт ит ланч бифор твелв пи эм]
Ular 12:00 gacha tushlik qilmaydilar.
Ovqat vaqti: Kun tartibi
He doesn't water plants in the evening.
[Хи дазнт вотэр плэнтс ин зэ ивнинг]
U kechqurun o'simliklarni sug'ormaydi.
Ferma ishi: Vaqt tartibi

3. So'roq shakl (Question Form)

Qachon ishlatamiz: Ishlar va odatlar haqida savol berishda

Qanday yasaymiz: Do/Does + subject + asosiy fe'l

Do/Does Subject Verb O'zbek ma'nosi
Do you/we/they work? ishlaysizmi?
Does he/she/it work? ishlaydimi?
Amaliy misollar (Practical Examples):
What do you do at work?
[Вот ду ю ду эт ворк?]
Ishda nima qilasiz?
Ish vazifasi: Umumiy savol
Do you pick strawberries or raspberries?
[Ду ю пик стробериз ор разбериз?]
Qulupnay terasizmi yoki malina terasizmi?
Ish turi: Tanlov savoli
Does she help you with heavy work?
[Даз ши хэлп ю виз хэви ворк?]
U sizga og'ir ishda yordam beradimi?
Yordam: Hamkorlik haqida
How many hours do we work today?
[Хау мени ауэрз ду ви ворк тудей?]
Bugun necha soat ishlaymiz?
Ish vaqti: Kunlik reja
Do they water plants every morning?
[Ду зей вотэр плэнтс эври морнинг?]
Ular har kuni ertalab o'simliklarni sug'oradimi?
Kun tartibi: Odatlar haqida

Amaliy qo'llash (Practical Application)

Mavsumiy ishchilar uchun real vaziyatlar:

🌱 Kun tartibini tushuntirish (Explaining daily routine):

Yangi ishchi: "What do you do every day at work?"

Siz: "I wake up at 6 AM, eat breakfast, and start work at 7. I pick strawberries until lunch time."

Yangi ishchi: "Ishda har kuni nima qilasiz?" Siz: "Men 6 da uyg'onaman, nonushta qilaman va 7 da ishni boshlayman. Tushlik vaqtigacha qulupnay teraman."

🤝 Ish vazifalarini taqsimlash (Dividing work tasks):

Supervayzer: "Do you carry heavy boxes or do you prefer lighter work?"

Siz: "I carry boxes, but she doesn't. She plants vegetables and waters the plants."

Supervayzer: "Og'ir qutilarni ko'tarasizmi yoki yengilroq ishni afzal ko'rasizmi?" Siz: "Men qutilarni ko'taraman, lekin u ko'tarmaydi. U sabzavot ekadi va o'simliklarni sug'oradi."

⏰ Ish vaqtlari haqida (About work hours):

Hamkasb: "Does he work overtime on weekends?"

Siz: "No, he doesn't work overtime. We only work regular hours from Monday to Friday."

Hamkasb: "U dam olish kunlari qo'shimcha vaqt ishlaydimi?" Siz: "Yo'q, u qo'shimcha vaqt ishlamaydi. Biz faqat dushanbadan jumagacha oddiy vaqt ishlaymiz."

🍽️ Ovqat vaqtlari (Meal times):

Siz: "What time do we eat lunch? Do you bring food from home?"

Hamkasb: "We eat at 12:30. I bring sandwiches, but some people buy food from the canteen."

Siz: "Necha vaqtda tushlik qilamiz? Uydan ovqat olib kelasizmi?" Hamkasb: "12:30 da ovqat qilamiz. Men sendvich olib kelaman, lekin ba'zi odamlar oshxonadan ovqat sotib olishadi."

Tez-tez qilinadigan xatolar (Common Mistakes)

❌ Xato (Wrong):

"He pick strawberries"

✅ To'g'ri (Correct):

"He picks strawberries"

Tushuntirish: He/She/It bilan fe'lga -s qo'shiladi.

❌ Xato (Wrong):

"I don't works on Sunday"

✅ To'g'ri (Correct):

"I don't work on Sunday"

Tushuntirish: "Don't" dan keyin fe'lning asosiy shakli keladi.

❌ Xato (Wrong):

"Does you carry boxes?"

✅ To'g'ri (Correct):

"Do you carry boxes?"

Tushuntirish: "You" bilan "Do" ishlatiladi, "Does" emas.

❌ Xato (Wrong):

"She doesn't picks fruit"

✅ To'g'ri (Correct):

"She doesn't pick fruit"

Tushuntirish: "Doesn't" dan keyin fe'lning asosiy shakli keladi.

❌ Xato (Wrong):

"What you do at work?"

✅ To'g'ri (Correct):

"What do you do at work?"

Tushuntirish: Savolda "do" yoki "does" ishlatish kerak.

Exercises / Mashqlar

1. Kun tartibi: Bo'sh joyni to'ldiring (Daily routine: Fill in the blanks)
Namuna (Example): I _____ up at 6 AM → I wake up at 6 AM

Complete: "Every morning, I _____ strawberries and _____ them in boxes." (pick/put)
2. Ish vazifalarini tushunish (Work task comprehension)
Namuna (Example): Supervisor says "Clean the tools" → You understand "Asboblarni tozalang"

Translate: "Men har kuni sabzavotlarni sug'oraman" → "I _____ vegetables every day."
3. Fe'l shakllari (Verb conjugation)
Namuna (Example): I carry → He carries (3rd person + s)

Complete: "She _____ heavy boxes, but I _____ light ones." (carry/carry)
4. So'roq va javob (Question and answer formation)
Namuna (Example): You work → Do you work?

Make a question: "He helps new workers" → "_____ he _____ new workers?"
5. Ish joyida muloqot (Workplace communication)
Complete: "I usually _____ at 6 AM, _____ breakfast, and _____ to work." (wake up/eat/go)
6. Inkor shakli (Negative form)
Namuna (Example): I work → I don't work

Make negative: "We plant vegetables on Sundays" → "We _____ _____ vegetables on Sundays."
7. Supervayzer bilan gaplashish (Talking with supervisor)
Complete: "Supervisor, I _____ the greenhouse every morning and _____ the plants." (clean/water)
8. Tarjima mashqi (Translation practice)
Translate: "U har kuni eshikni ochadi va yopadi" → "He _____ and _____ the door every day."
9. Ish vaqti haqida so'rash (Asking about work time)
Complete the conversation: "_____ _____ you start work?" "I start at 7 AM."
10. Amaliy vazifa: Ish kunini tasvirlash (Practical task: Describe work day)
Complete: "Every day I _____ up early, _____ to the farm, and _____ until evening." (wake/walk/work)
