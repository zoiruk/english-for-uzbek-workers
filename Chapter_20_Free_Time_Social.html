Grammar / Grammatika

Bu grammatika nima uchun kerak? (Why do we need this grammar?)

Gerund va Infinitive - bu ingliz tilida fe'llardan keyin keladigan boshqa fe'llarning shakllari. Bu juda muhim, chunki siz bo'sh vaqtingizda nima qilishni yoqtirishingiz, nima qilishni xohlashingiz haqida to'g'ri gapirish uchun kerak. Masalan, "Men kitob o'qishni yaxshi ko'raman" yoki "Men sayohat qilishni xohlayman" deyish uchun bu qoidalarni bilish shart.

Asosiy tushuncha (Basic Concept)

Gerund (-ing shakli): Fe'lga -ing qo'shib, ot sifatida ishlatish. Masalan: swimming, reading, dancing.

Infinitive (to + fe'l): Fe'l oldiga "to" qo'yib ishlatish. Masalan: to swim, to read, to dance.

Qachon qaysi birini ishlatish: Ba'zi fe'llar faqat gerund, ba'zilari faqat infinitive, ba'zilari ikkalasini ham qabul qiladi.

Amaliy qo'llash (Practical Application)

1. Bo'sh vaqt faoliyatlari haqida gaplashish (Talking about leisure activities)

Vaziyat: Yangi do'stlar bilan tanishayotganda

Gerund misollar:

• "I enjoy reading books in English." - Men ingliz tilida kitob o'qishdan zavqlanaman.

• "She loves cooking traditional food." - U an'anaviy taom pishirishni yaxshi ko'radi.

Infinitive misollar:

• "I want to learn British culture." - Men Britaniya madaniyatini o'rganishni xohlayman.

• "We plan to visit London museums." - Biz London muzeylariga tashrif buyurishni rejalashtirmoqdamiz.

2. Takliflar qilish va qabul qilish (Making and accepting invitations)

Vaziyat: Hamkasblar bilan dam olish rejasi tuzish

Taklif qilish:

• "Do you enjoy watching football?" - Futbol ko'rishdan zavqlanasizmi?

• "Would you like to try playing games?" - O'yin o'ynashni sinab ko'rishni xohlaysizmi?

Javob berish:

• "I love watching movies!" - Men film ko'rishni yaxshi ko'raman!

• "I don't really enjoy dancing." - Men raqsga tushishni unchalik yoqtirmayman.

3. Rejalar va orzu-havaslar (Plans and dreams)

Vaziyat: Kelajak rejalari haqida suhbat

Rejalar (Infinitive):

• "I hope to travel around Britain." - Men Britaniya bo'ylab sayohat qilishga umid qilaman.

• "She wants to learn traditional British dances." - U an'anaviy Britaniya raqslarini o'rganishni xohlaydi.

Hozirgi qiziqishlar (Gerund):

• "I enjoy meeting people from different countries." - Men turli mamlakatlardan odamlar bilan uchrashishdan zavqlanaman.

Tez-tez qilinadigan xatolar (Common Mistakes)

❌ Xato (Wrong) ➡️ ✅ To'g'ri (Correct)

1. Noto'g'ri shakl tanlash:

❌ "I like to swimming" ➡️ ✅ "I like swimming" (gerund)

❌ "I want dancing" ➡️ ✅ "I want to dance" (infinitive)

Tushuntirish: "Like" dan keyin gerund, "want" dan keyin infinitive keladi.

2. Gerund shaklini noto'g'ri yasash:

❌ "I enjoy to dance" ➡️ ✅ "I enjoy dancing"

❌ "She loves to read" ➡️ ✅ "She loves reading"

Tushuntirish: "Enjoy" va "love" dan keyin gerund (-ing) keladi.

3. Infinitive da "to" ni unutish:

❌ "I want go home" ➡️ ✅ "I want to go home"

❌ "She plans visit London" ➡️ ✅ "She plans to visit London"

Tushuntirish: Infinitive da "to" majburiy.

4. Ikki shaklni aralashtirib yuborish:

❌ "I like to swimming and to dance" ➡️ ✅ "I like swimming and dancing"

❌ "I want dancing and singing" ➡️ ✅ "I want to dance and sing"

Tushuntirish: Bir xil fe'l uchun bir xil shakl ishlatish kerak.
