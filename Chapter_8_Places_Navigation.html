Grammar / Grammatika

Grammatika - Prepositions of Place and Direction (Joy va Yo'nalish Predloglari)

Bu grammatika nima uchun kerak? (Why do we need this grammar?)

Joy va yo'nalish predloglarini bilish mavsumiy ishchilar uchun juda muhim. Siz har kuni yangi joylarni topishingiz, yo'l so'rashingiz va boshqalarga yo'nalish berishingiz kerak bo'ladi. Masalan: "Bank pochta yonida" yoki "To'g'ri boring, keyin chapga burilib". Bu predloglar sizga shaharni yaxshi bilishga va kerakli joylarni topishga yordam beradi.

Asosiy tushuncha (Basic Concept)

Predloglar - bu joyning o'rnini va yo'nalishni ko'rsatuvchi kichik so'zlar. Ular ikki turga bo'linadi: Joy predloglari (nima qayerda ekanligini ko'rsatadi) va Yo'nalish predloglari (qayerga borishni ko'rsatadi). Ingliz tilida bu predloglar juda aniq ma'noga ega va ularni to'g'ri ishlatish muhim.

Uch shakl (Three Forms)

1. Joy predloglari (Prepositions of Place)

Preposition Meaning Usage
in ichida in the town centre
on ustida on High Street
at -da/-de at the bus stop
next to yonida next to the bank
opposite qarshisida opposite the post office
between orasida between the shops

Misollar (Examples):

The GP surgery is in the town centre.
[Зэ джи пи сёрджери из ин зэ таун сентэр]
Shifokor kabineti shahar markazida.
Tibbiy yordam: Shifokor qayerda ekanligini aytish
The pharmacy is next to the supermarket.
[Зэ фармаси из некст ту зэ супермаркет]
Dorixona supermarket yonida.
Xarid: Dorixonani topish
The bank is opposite the post office.
[Зэ бэнк из опозит зэ пост офис]
Bank pochta qarshisida.
Moliya: Bank joylashuvini aytish
The bus stop is at the corner.
[Зэ бас стоп из эт зэ корнэр]
Avtobus bekati burchakda.
Transport: Bekat joylashuvi
My workplace is between two farms.
[Май воркплэйс из битвин ту фармс]
Mening ish joyim ikki ferma orasida.
Ish: Ish joyi haqida ma'lumot

2. Yo'nalish predloglari (Prepositions of Direction)

Preposition Meaning Usage
to -ga/-ka go to the bank
towards tomon walk towards the station
along bo'ylab walk along the street
across orqali go across the road
past yonidan o'tib go past the church
through orqali go through the park

Misollar (Examples):

Go straight to the traffic lights.
[Гоу стрэйт ту зэ трафик лайтс]
To'g'ri svetoforgacha boring.
Yo'nalish: Asosiy yo'l ko'rsatish
Walk along High Street for 200 metres.
[Волк элонг хай стрит фор ту хандред митэрс]
High Street bo'ylab 200 metr yuring.
Masofa: Aniq yo'nalish berish
Go across the road at the zebra crossing.
[Гоу экрос зэ роуд эт зэ зебра кросинг]
Zebra yo'lida ko'chani kesib o'ting.
Xavfsizlik: Xavfsiz o'tish
Walk past the church and turn right.
[Волк паст зэ чёрч энд тёрн райт]
Cherkov yonidan o'tib, o'ngga burilib.
Orientir: Bino orqali yo'nalish
Go through the park to reach the hospital.
[Гоу сру зэ парк ту рич зэ хоспитал]
Kasalxonaga borish uchun park orqali o'ting.
Qisqa yo'l: Tezroq yetish

3. Yo'l so'rash va berish (Asking and Giving Directions)

Question Words Structure Response Pattern
Where is...? Where is + place? It's + preposition + place
How do I get to...? How do I get to + place? Go/Walk/Turn + direction

Misollar (Examples):

A: Where is the nearest GP surgery?
[Вэр из зэ ниэрест джи пи сёрджери?]
Eng yaqin shifokor kabineti qayerda?
B: It's next to the pharmacy on High Street.
[Итс некст ту зэ фармаси он хай стрит]
High Streetdagi dorixona yonida.
Tibbiy yordam: Shifokor topish
A: How do I get to the job centre?
[Хау ду ай гет ту зэ джоб сентэр?]
Ish markaziga qanday boraman?
B: Go straight, then turn left at the roundabout.
[Гоу стрэйт, зэн тёрн лефт эт зэ раундэбаут]
To'g'ri boring, keyin aylanada chapga burilib.
Ish: Muhim joylarni topish
A: Excuse me, where is the bus station?
[Экскюз ми, вэр из зэ бас стэйшн?]
Uzr so'rayman, avtobus vokzali qayerda?
B: It's opposite the shopping centre.
[Итс опозит зэ шопинг сентэр]
Savdo markazi qarshisida.
Transport: Vokzal topish
A: Is the bank far from here?
[Из зэ бэнк фар фром хиэр?]
Bank bu yerdan uzoqmi?
B: No, it's just around the corner.
[Ноу, итс джаст эраунд зэ корнэр]
Yo'q, faqat burchak ortida.
Masofa: Yaqinlik haqida
A: Can you show me the way to the farm?
[Кэн ю шоу ми зэ вэй ту зэ фарм?]
Fermaga yo'lni ko'rsata olasizmi?
B: Walk along this road for 10 minutes.
[Волк элонг зис роуд фор тен минитс]
Bu yo'l bo'ylab 10 daqiqa yuring.
Ish joyi: Fermani topish

Amaliy qo'llash (Practical Application)

Mavsumiy ishchilar uchun muhim vaziyatlar:

  • Ish joyini topish: "The farm is at the end of this road" - yangi ish joyiga borish
  • Tibbiy yordam: "The GP surgery is next to the pharmacy" - shifokor topish
  • Xarid qilish: "The supermarket is opposite the bus stop" - do'kon topish
  • Transport: "Walk to the bus stop and turn right" - bekat topish
  • Moliya: "The bank is between the post office and the library" - bank topish
  • Yordam berish: "Go straight, then turn left at the traffic lights" - boshqalarga yo'l ko'rsatish

Tez-tez qilinadigan xatolar (Common Mistakes)

❌ Xato: The bank is in the post office
✅ To'g'ri: The bank is next to the post office
"In" ichkarida, "next to" yonida demakdir
❌ Xato: Go to straight
✅ To'g'ri: Go straight
"Straight" oldida "to" kerak emas
❌ Xato: Turn to the left
✅ To'g'ri: Turn left
Yo'nalishda "to" ishlatilmaydi
❌ Xato: It's on the corner from the street
✅ To'g'ri: It's on the corner of the street
Burchak uchun "of" ishlatiladi
❌ Xato: Walk by the road
✅ To'g'ri: Walk along the road
Yo'l bo'ylab yurish uchun "along" ishlatiladi

Dialogues / Suhbatlar

Tourist:
Excuse me, where is the post office?
Uzr so'rayman, pochta qayerda?
Local:
Go straight ahead, then turn left at the traffic lights.
To'g'ri boring, keyin svetoforda chapga burilib.
Worker:
How do I get to the GP surgery?
Shifokor kabinetiga qanday boraman?
Receptionist:
It's opposite the pharmacy, next to the supermarket.
U dorixona qarshisida, supermarket yonida.
Visitor:
Is the bank far from here?
Bank bu yerdan uzoqmi?
Local:
No, it's very close. About 2 minutes' walk.
Yo'q, juda yaqin. Taxminan 2 daqiqa piyoda.
Lost Person:
I'm looking for the hospital. Can you help me?
Men kasalxonani qidiryapman. Yordam bera olasizmi?
Helpful Person:
Yes, go to the roundabout and take the second exit.
Ha, aylanmaga boring va ikkinchi chiqishni oling.
Shopper:
Where can I find a good supermarket?
Yaxshi supermarketni qayerdan topishim mumkin?
Local:
There's a big Tesco on the high street.
Asosiy ko'chada katta Tesco bor.
Worker:
Is there a car park near the town centre?
Shahar markazi yaqinida avtoturargoh bormi?
Information:
Yes, there's one behind the library.
Ha, kutubxona orqasida bittasi bor.
Tourist:
How long does it take to walk to the park?
Parkgacha piyoda qancha vaqt ketadi?
Local:
About 10 minutes. Cross the bridge and it's on your left.
Taxminan 10 daqiqa. Ko'prikni kesib o'ting va chap tomoningizda.
Visitor:
I need to find the police station urgently.
Politsiya bo'limini shoshilinch topishim kerak.
Passerby:
It's at the corner of High Street and Church Road.
U High Street va Church Road burchagida.
New Resident:
Where's the nearest pharmacy?
Eng yaqin dorixona qayerda?
Neighbor:
Turn right at the crossroads. It's between the bank and the café.
Chorrahada o'ngga burilib. U bank va kafe orasida.
Student:
Can you show me on the map where we are?
Xaritada qayerda ekanligimizni ko'rsata olasizmi?
Helper:
We're here, in front of the church. The library is over there.
Biz bu yerdamiz, cherkov oldida. Kutubxona u yerda.
