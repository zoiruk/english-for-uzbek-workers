Grammar / Grammatika

Grammatika - "To Be" fe'li (am/is/are)

Bu grammatika nima uchun kerak? (Why do we need this grammar?)

Ingliz tilida "to be" fe'li eng muhim fe'ldir. Bu fe'l yordamida siz o'zingiz haqingizda, boshqalar haqida va narsalar haqida gapirasiz. Mavsumiy ishchi sifatida bu fe'lni har kuni ishlatasiz - o'zingizni tanishtirishda, ish joyida, turar joyda va do'konlarda.

Misol: "Men mavsumiy ishchiman" - "I am a seasonal worker"

Asosiy tushuncha (Basic Concept)

O'zbek tilida "Men ishchiman" deb aytganda, ingliz tilida "I am a worker" deb aytamiz. "Am" - bu "to be" fe'lining bir qismi. Har bir shaxs uchun alohida so'z ishlatiladi:

  • I (Men) bilan doimo am ishlatiladi
  • You (Siz) bilan doimo are ishlatiladi
  • He/She/It (U) bilan doimo is ishlatiladi
  • We (Biz) bilan doimo are ishlatiladi
  • They (Ular) bilan doimo are ishlatiladi

Uch shakl (Three Forms)

1. Tasdiqlovchi shakl (Positive Form)

Qachon ishlatamiz: Biror narsa haqida ijobiy ma'lumot berishda

Subject (Kim?) To Be Information O'zbek tilidagi ma'nosi
I am a worker Men ishchiman
You are from Uzbekistan Siz O'zbekistondansiz
He/She is my supervisor U mening supervayzerim
Amaliy misollar (Practical Examples):
I am a seasonal worker.
[Ай эм э сизонал воркер]
Men mavsumiy ishchiman.
Ishda: O'zingizni tanishtirishda
You are from Tashkent.
[Ю ар фром Ташкент]
Siz Toshkentdansiz.
Turar joyda: Yangi odamlar bilan tanishishda
She is my roommate.
[Ши из май румейт]
U mening xonadoshim.
Turar joyda: Boshqalarga tanishtirishda
We are ready to work.
[Ви ар реди ту ворк]
Biz ishlashga tayyormiz.
Ishda: Supervayzerga holatni bildirish
They are new workers.
[Зей ар нью воркерз]
Ular yangi ishchilar.
Ishda: Boshqalar haqida ma'lumot berish

Qachon ishlatamiz: Biror narsa haqida savol berishda

Qanday yasaymiz: "to be" ni gapning boshiga qo'yamiz

Amaliy qo'llash (Practical Application)

Mavsumiy ishchilar uchun real vaziyatlar:

🏢 Ishga birinchi kun (First day at work):

Supervayzer: "Are you the new worker from Uzbekistan?"

Siz: "Yes, I am. I am ready to work."

Supervayzer: "Siz O'zbekistondan yangi ishchimisiz?" Siz: "Ha, men. Men ishlashga tayyorman."

🏠 Turar joyda tanishish (Meeting at accommodation):

Xonadosh: "Are you from Tashkent?"

Siz: "No, I am not from Tashkent. I am from Samarkand."

Xonadosh: "Siz Toshkentdanmisiz?" Siz: "Yo'q, men Toshkentdan emasman. Men Samarqanddanman."

🛒 Do'konda yordam so'rash (Asking for help in shop):

Siz: "Excuse me, are you the manager?"

Xodim: "No, I am not the manager. She is over there."

Siz: "Uzr so'rayman, siz menedjermisinz?" Xodim: "Yo'q, men menejer emasman. U ana u yerda."

🚌 Transportda (On transport):

Siz: "Is this the bus to the farm?"

Haydovchi: "Yes, it is. Are you a seasonal worker?"

Siz: "Bu fermaga boradigan avtobusmi?" Haydovchi: "Ha, to'g'ri. Siz mavsumiy ishchimisiz?"

Tez-tez qilinadigan xatolar (Common Mistakes)

❌ Xato (Wrong):

"I is from Uzbekistan"

✅ To'g'ri (Correct):

"I am from Uzbekistan"

Tushuntirish: "I" bilan doimo "am" ishlatiladi, "is" emas.

❌ Xato (Wrong):

"You is ready"

✅ To'g'ri (Correct):

"You are ready"

Tushuntirish: "You" bilan doimo "are" ishlatiladi.

❌ Xato (Wrong):

"He are my friend"

✅ To'g'ri (Correct):

"He is my friend"

Tushuntirish: "He/She/It" bilan doimo "is" ishlatiladi.

❌ Xato (Wrong):

"Are you from where?"

✅ To'g'ri (Correct):

"Where are you from?"

Tushuntirish: Savol so'zini gapning boshiga qo'yamiz.

❌ Xato (Wrong):

"We is workers"

✅ To'g'ri (Correct):

"We are workers"

Tushuntirish: "We/They" bilan doimo "are" ishlatiladi.

Exercises / Mashqlar

1. Bo'sh joyni to'ldiring (Fill in the blanks)
Namuna (Example): I _____ a seasonal worker. → I am a seasonal worker.

Complete with the correct form of "to be" (am/is/are): "I _____ from Uzbekistan."
2. O'zbek tilidan inglizchaga tarjima qiling (Translate from Uzbek to English)
Namuna (Example): Men mavsumiy ishchiman → I am a seasonal worker

Translate: "U mening supervayzerim" (Use "He is...")
3. To'g'ri javobni tanlang (Choose the correct answer)
Which is correct for "Biz tayyormiz" (We are ready)?
Type: a) We am ready    b) We is ready    c) We are ready
4. Gapni to'g'ri tartibda yozing (Write the sentence in correct order)
Namuna (Example): [worker, am, seasonal, I, a] → I am a seasonal worker

Put in order: [from, are, Tashkent, You]
5. Inkorlovchi shaklni yarating (Make it negative)
Namuna (Example): I am tired → I am not tired

Make negative: "She is my roommate" → "She _____ my roommate"
6. So'roq gapni yarating (Make a question)
Namuna (Example): You are ready → Are you ready?

Make a question: "They are new workers" → "_____ they new workers?"
7. Ish joyida: Bo'sh joyni to'ldiring (Workplace: Fill in the blanks)
Complete this workplace introduction: "Hello, I _____ Akmal and I _____ a seasonal worker."
8. Tarjima qiling (Translate)
Workplace situation: "Ular yangi ishchilar" → "_____ _____ new workers"
9. O'zingiz haqingizda yozing (Write about yourself)
Complete this self-introduction: "My name _____ [your name] and I _____ from Uzbekistan."
10. Amaliy vazifa (Practical task)
You meet your supervisor. Complete: "Good morning! I _____ ready to work. My passport _____ here."
