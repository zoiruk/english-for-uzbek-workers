Grammar / Grammatika

Bu grammatika nima uchun kerak? (Why do we need this grammar?)

Present Perfect zamoni sizga hayotiy tajribalaringiz va amalga oshirgan ishlaringiz haqida gapirish imkonini beradi. Bu zamon juda muhim, chunki siz boshqa mamlakatlardan kelgan ishchilar bilan o'z tajribalaringizni baham ko'rishingiz kerak bo'ladi. Masalan, "Men Londonda bo'lganman" yoki "Men bu ishni qilganman" deyishingiz mumkin.

Asosiy tushuncha (Basic Concept)

Present Perfect - bu o'tmishda sodir bo'lgan, lekin hozirgi vaqt bilan bog'liq bo'lgan ishlar uchun ishlatiladi. Bu zamon ikki holatda ishlatiladi:

1. Hayotiy tajriba (Life experience): "Men Shotlandiyada bo'lganman" - aniq vaqt muhim emas, tajriba muhim.

2. Tugallangan ish (Completed action): "Men ishni tugatganman" - ish tugagan, natija hozir muhim.

Uch shakl (Three Forms)

1. Tasdiqlovchi shakl (Positive Form)

Tuzilishi: Subject + have/has + Past Participle (3rd form)

Subject have/has Past Participle O'zbek tarjima
I/You/We/Theyhavevisitedtashrif buyurganman/sz/miz/lar
He/She/Ithasvisitedtashrif buyurgan
I have visited London.
[Ай хэв визитид Ландэн]
Men Londonga tashrif buyurganman.
Hayotiy tajriba: Londonda bo'lganligingizni aytish
She has worked in many countries.
[Ши хэз воркт ин мэни кантриз]
U ko'p mamlakatlarda ishlagan.
Ish tajribasi: Xalqaro ish tajribasi haqida
We have learned English.
[Уи хэв лёрнд инглиш]
Biz ingliz tilini o'rganganmiz.
Ta'lim: O'rganilgan tillar haqida
They have traveled to Europe.
[Зэй хэв трэвэлд ту Юэроп]
Ular Yevropaga sayohat qilganlar.
Sayohat: Safar tajribasi haqida
He has met people from different cultures.
[Хи хэз мет пипл фром дифрэнт калчэз]
U turli madaniyatlardan odamlar bilan uchrashgan.
Ijtimoiy: Madaniyatlararo muloqot

Tuzilishi: Have/Has + subject + Past Participle?

[Хэз ши воркт уиз интэнэшнэл тимз?]
U xalqaro jamoalar bilan ishlaganmi?

[Хэв ю лёрнд эни нью лэнгвиджиз?]
Siz biron-bir yangi til o'rganganmisiz?
Ta'lim: Til o'rganish tajribasi
Has he made friends from other countries?
[Хэз хи мэйд фрэндз фром азэ кантриз?]
U boshqa mamlakatlardan do'stlar orttirganmi?
Ijtimoiy: Xalqaro do'stlik

Amaliy qo'llash (Practical Application)

1. Hayotiy tajriba baham ko'rish (Sharing Life Experience)

Vaziyat: Boshqa ishchilar bilan suhbatlashayotganda

Misollar:

• "I have worked in three different countries." - Men uch xil mamlakatda ishlaganman.

• "Have you ever been to London?" - Siz hech qachon Londonda bo'lganmisiz?

• "She has visited many European cities." - U ko'plab Yevropa shaharlarini ko'rgan.

2. Madaniy tajriba muhokamasi (Cultural Experience Discussion)

Vaziyat: Turli madaniyatlar haqida gaplashish

Misollar:

• "I have experienced British hospitality." - Men Britaniya mehmondo'stligini his qilganman.

• "Have you tried traditional British food?" - Siz an'anaviy Britaniya taomlarini tatib ko'rganmisiz?

• "We have learned about local customs." - Biz mahalliy urf-odatlar haqida bilib oldik.

3. Til o'rganish muhokamasi (Language Learning Discussion)

Vaziyat: Til bilish darajasi haqida gaplashish

Misollar:

• "I have studied English for two years." - Men ikki yil ingliz tilini o'rganganman.

• "Has he improved his pronunciation?" - U talaffuzini yaxshilaganmi?

• "They have practiced speaking every day." - Ular har kuni gaplashishni mashq qilganlar.

Tez-tez qilinadigan xatolar (Common Mistakes)

❌ Xato (Wrong) ➡️ ✅ To'g'ri (Correct)

1. Noto'g'ri yordamchi fe'l:

❌ "I am visited London" ➡️ ✅ "I have visited London"

Tushuntirish: Present Perfect da "have/has" ishlatiladi, "am/is/are" emas.

2. Past Simple bilan aralashtirib yuborish:

❌ "I have visited London yesterday" ➡️ ✅ "I visited London yesterday" (Past Simple)

❌ "I visited London" (aniq vaqtsiz) ➡️ ✅ "I have visited London" (Present Perfect)

Tushuntirish: Aniq vaqt bilan Past Simple, tajriba uchun Present Perfect ishlatiladi.

3. Past Participle shaklini bilmaslik:

❌ "I have go to Scotland" ➡️ ✅ "I have been to Scotland"

❌ "She has work here" ➡️ ✅ "She has worked here"

Tushuntirish: Fe'lning 3-shaklini (Past Participle) ishlatish kerak.

4. "Ever" so'zini noto'g'ri ishlatish:

❌ "Have you been ever to Wales?" ➡️ ✅ "Have you ever been to Wales?"

Tushuntirish: "Ever" so'zi subject va past participle orasida keladi.

Namuna: Have you _____ been to Scotland? (ever) → Have you ever been to Scotland?

Exercise 4: To'g'ri javobni tanlang (Choose the correct answer) - Experience vs Specific Time
