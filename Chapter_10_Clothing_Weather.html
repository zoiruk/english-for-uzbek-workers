Grammar / Grammatika

Grammatika - Present Continuous for Current Actions (Hozirgi Davomli Zamon)

Bu grammatika nima uchun kerak? (Why do we need this grammar?)

Present Continuous zamonini hozir sodir bo'layotgan ishlar haqida gapirish uchun ishlatamiz. Bu zamon mavsumiy ishchilar uchun juda muhim, chunki siz har kuni hozir nima qilayotganingizni, qanday kiyinganingizni, ob-havo qanday ekanligini aytishingiz kerak. Masalan: "Men hozir kurtka kiyganman" yoki "Yomg'ir yog'yapti". Bu Present Simple dan farqli - Present Simple odatiy ishlar uchun, Present Continuous esa hozir sodir bo'layotgan ishlar uchun.

Asosiy tushuncha (Basic Concept)

Present Continuous zamonini yasash uchun am/is/are + fe'l + ing formulasidan foydalanamiz. Bu zamon hozir sodir bo'layotgan ishlarni ko'rsatadi. Masalan: "I am working" (men hozir ishlayapman), "It is raining" (hozir yomg'ir yog'yapti). Bu zamon bilan vaqt ko'rsatkichlari ishlatiladi: now (hozir), at the moment (ayni damda), today (bugun), this week (bu hafta).

Uch shakl (Three Forms)

1. Tasdiqlovchi shakl (Positive Form)

Subject am/is/are Verb + ing Object/Place
I am wearing a jacket
He/She/It is raining outside
You/We/They are working in the field

Misollar (Examples):

I am wearing warm clothes today.
[Ай эм вэринг ворм клоузс тудэй]
Men bugun issiq kiyim kiyganman.
Kiyim: Hozirgi kiyim haqida
It is raining heavily right now.
[Ит из рэйнинг хэвили райт нау]
Hozir qattiq yomg'ir yog'yapti.
Ob-havo: Joriy holat
We are working in the greenhouse today.
[Ви ар воркинг ин зэ гринхаус тудэй]
Biz bugun issiqxonada ishlayapmiz.
Ish: Bugungi ish joyi
She is buying winter boots at the moment.
[Ши из байинг винтэр бутс эт зэ моумэнт]
U ayni damda qishki etik sotib olayapti.
Xarid: Joriy faoliyat
The weather is getting colder this week.
[Зэ вэзэр из гэтинг коулдэр зис вик]
Bu hafta ob-havo sovuyapti.
O'zgarish: Ob-havo o'zgarishi

2. Inkorlovchi shakl (Negative Form)

Subject am/is/are + not Verb + ing Object/Place
I am not wearing a coat
He/She/It is not (isn't) snowing today
You/We/They are not (aren't) working outside

Misollar (Examples):

I am not wearing a hat today.
[Ай эм нот вэринг э хэт тудэй]
Men bugun shlyapa kiymagan.
Kiyim: Nima kiymaganini aytish
It isn't snowing at the moment.
[Ит изнт сноуинг эт зэ моумэнт]
Ayni damda qor yog'mayapti.
Ob-havo: Nima bo'lmayotgani
We aren't working in the field today.
[Ви арнт воркинг ин зэ филд тудэй]
Biz bugun dalada ishlamayapmiz.
Ish: Bugungi ish joyini inkor qilish
She isn't buying summer clothes now.
[Ши изнт байинг самэр клоузс нау]
U hozir yozgi kiyim sotib olmayapti.
Xarid: Nima qilmayotgani
The temperature isn't rising this week.
[Зэ тэмпэрэчэр изнт райзинг зис вик]
Bu hafta harorat ko'tarilmayapti.
Holat: O'zgarish yo'qligi

3. So'roq shakl (Question Form)

Am/Is/Are Subject Verb + ing Object/Place?
Am I wearing enough clothes?
Is it raining outside?
Are you working today?

Misollar (Examples):

A: Are you wearing warm clothes?
[Ар ю вэринг ворм клоузс?]
Issiq kiyim kiyganmisiz?
B: Yes, I am wearing a thick jacket.
[Йес, ай эм вэринг э сик джэкит]
Ha, qalin kurtka kiyganman.
G'amxo'rlik: Kiyim haqida so'rash
A: Is it raining outside?
[Из ит рэйнинг аутсайд?]
Tashqarida yomg'ir yog'yapti?
B: No, it's just cloudy.
[Ноу, итс джаст клауди]
Yo'q, faqat bulutli.
Ob-havo: Joriy holat haqida
A: What are you doing right now?
[Вот ар ю дуинг райт нау?]
Hozir nima qilayapsiz?
B: I'm getting ready for work.
[Айм гэтинг рэди фор ворк]
Ishga tayyorlanayapman.
Faoliyat: Hozirgi ish haqida
A: Are we working inside today?
[Ар ви воркинг инсайд тудэй?]
Bugun ichkarida ishlaymizmi?
B: Yes, because of the bad weather.
[Йес, бикоз ов зэ бэд вэзэр]
Ha, yomon ob-havo tufayli.
Ish rejasi: Bugungi ish joyi
A: Is the weather changing this week?
[Из зэ вэзэр чэйнджинг зис вик?]
Bu hafta ob-havo o'zgarayaptimi?
B: Yes, it's getting warmer.
[Йес, итс гэтинг вормэр]
Ha, isinayapti.
Prognoz: Ob-havo o'zgarishi

Amaliy qo'llash (Practical Application)

Mavsumiy ishchilar uchun muhim vaziyatlar:

  • Ish joyida: "I am working in the greenhouse today" - bugungi ish joyi haqida
  • Ob-havo: "It's raining, so we're staying inside" - joriy ob-havo ta'siri
  • Kiyim: "I'm wearing waterproof boots" - hozirgi kiyim haqida
  • Rejalar: "We're changing our work schedule" - joriy o'zgarishlar
  • Muammolar: "The heating isn't working properly" - joriy muammolar
  • Tayyorgarlik: "I'm getting ready for the cold season" - kelayotgan o'zgarishlarga tayyorlik

Tez-tez qilinadigan xatolar (Common Mistakes)

❌ Xato: I wearing a coat
✅ To'g'ri: I am wearing a coat
Present Continuous da doimo am/is/are kerak
❌ Xato: It raining outside
✅ To'g'ri: It is raining outside
Ob-havo uchun ham "is" kerak
❌ Xato: I am wear a jacket
✅ To'g'ri: I am wearing a jacket
Fe'lga doimo -ing qo'shimchasi qo'shiladi
❌ Xato: Are you wear warm clothes?
✅ To'g'ri: Are you wearing warm clothes?
So'roq shaklida ham -ing kerak
❌ Xato: I am not wear a hat
✅ To'g'ri: I am not wearing a hat
Inkor shaklida ham -ing qo'shimchasi kerak

Exercises / Mashqlar

1. Hozirgi faoliyatni tasvirlash (Current activity description)
Namuna: I _____ wearing a warm coat today. (am/is/are) → am
Right now, I _____ my winter boots. (wear - Present Continuous)
2. Ob-havo va kiyim vaziyati (Weather and clothing situation)
Namuna: It's raining, so I'm carrying an umbrella
Look outside! It _____ heavily. (snow - Present Continuous)
3. Davomiy va odatiy harakatlar farqi (Ongoing vs habitual actions)
Namuna: I usually wear a coat (habit) vs I'm wearing a coat now (current)
Right now, I _____ the weather forecast. (check - Present Continuous)
4. Mavsumiy ish faoliyatini tasvirlash (Seasonal work activity description)
Namuna: We're working outside in cold weather today
All the workers _____ warm gloves today. (wear - Present Continuous)
5. O'zbek tilidan inglizchaga tarjima qiling (Translate current conditions)
Namuna: Hozir yomg'ir yog'moqda → It's raining now
Men sovuqlanib qolyapman → _____
6. Present Continuous so'roq gaplari (Present Continuous questions)
Namuna: You are wearing a coat → Are you wearing a coat?
It is raining outside → _____
7. Inkor Present Continuous (Negative Present Continuous)
Namuna: I'm wearing a hat → I'm not wearing a hat
The sun _____ today. (shine - negative Present Continuous)
8. Ish joyida ob-havo muloqoti (Workplace weather communication)
Namuna: The supervisor is telling us to wear warm clothes
The workers _____ into warmer clothes. (change - Present Continuous)
9. Hozirgi ob-havo sharoitlari (Current weather conditions)
Namuna: The temperature is dropping quickly
Havo issiqroq bo'lib bormoqda → The weather _____
