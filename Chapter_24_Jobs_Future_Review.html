Complete A0-A1 Grammar Overview / To'liq A0-A1 Grammatika Ko'rinishi

1. Essential Tenses / Asosiy Zamonlar

Present Simple: Daily routines, facts, permanent situations

O'zbek: Kundalik ishlar, faktlar, doimiy vaziyatlar

Workplace use: "I work on farms" / "Men fermalarda ishlayman"

Past Simple: Completed actions in the past

O'zbek: O'tmishda tugagan ishlar

Workplace use: "I worked in Uzbekistan for 5 years" / "Men O'zbekistonda 5 yil ishladim"

Present Continuous: Actions happening now or temporary situations

O'zbek: Hozir sodir bo'layotgan yoki vaqtinchalik vaziyatlar

Workplace use: "I am working on a farm this season" / "Men bu mavsumda fermada ishlayapman"

Present Perfect: Experience, results, unfinished time periods

O'zbek: Tajriba, natijalar, tugamagan vaqt davrlari

Workplace use: "I have worked with different machines" / "Men turli mashinalar bilan ishlaganman"

Future Forms (will/going to): Plans, predictions, decisions

O'zbek: Rejalar, bashoratlar, qarorlar

Workplace use: "I will return next year" / "Men kelgusi yil qaytaman"

2. Modal Verbs / Modal Fe'llar

Can/Could: Ability, possibility, polite requests

O'zbek: Qobiliyat, imkoniyat, iltimos so'rash

Workplace use: "I can operate machinery" / "Men texnikani boshqara olaman"

Must/Have to: Obligation, necessity

O'zbek: Majburiyat, zarurat

Workplace use: "I must follow safety rules" / "Men xavfsizlik qoidalariga amal qilishim shart"

Should: Advice, recommendations

O'zbek: Maslahat, tavsiya

Workplace use: "You should wear protective equipment" / "Siz himoya vositalarini kiyishingiz kerak"

Would like: Polite wants and preferences

O'zbek: Iltimos bilan xohish va afzallik

Workplace use: "I would like to learn new skills" / "Men yangi ko'nikmalar o'rganishni xohlayman"

3. Question Formation / So'roq Shakli Tuzish

Yes/No Questions: Do/Does, Did, Will, Can, Have/Has

O'zbek: Ha/Yo'q so'roqlari

Examples: "Do you have experience?" / "Sizda tajriba bormi?"

Wh-Questions: What, Where, When, Why, How, Who

O'zbek: Wh-so'roqlari

Examples: "Where did you work before?" / "Ilgari qayerda ishlagansiz?"

Question Tags: Confirming information

O'zbek: Ma'lumotni tasdiqlash

Examples: "You're from Uzbekistan, aren't you?" / "Siz O'zbekistonlikusiz, shunday emasmi?"

4. Comparison and Description / Solishtirish va Tavsif

Comparative Adjectives: -er, more + adjective

O'zbek: Solishtirma sifatlar

Examples: "This job is better than my previous one" / "Bu ish avvalgisidan yaxshiroq"

Superlative Adjectives: -est, most + adjective

O'zbek: Eng yuqori daraja sifatlari

Examples: "This is the best opportunity for me" / "Bu men uchun eng yaxshi imkoniyat"

Describing People and Things: Physical appearance, personality, objects

O'zbek: Odamlar va narsalarni tasvirlash

Examples: "My supervisor is very helpful" / "Mening rahbarim juda yordam beradi"

5. Possession and Relationships / Egalik va Munosabatlar

Possessive Adjectives: my, your, his, her, its, our, their

O'zbek: Egalik sifatlari

Examples: "My experience helps me in my work" / "Mening tajribam ishimda yordam beradi"

Possessive 's: Showing ownership

O'zbek: Egalikni ko'rsatish

Examples: "The company's policy is clear" / "Kompaniyaning siyosati aniq"

Family and Relationships: Describing connections

O'zbek: Oila va munosabatlarni tasvirlash

Examples: "My family supports my career" / "Mening oilam martabamni qo'llab-quvvatlaydi"

6. Verb Forms and Patterns / Fe'l Shakllari va Naqshlari

Gerunds (-ing forms): As subjects, after prepositions

O'zbek: -ing shakllari

Examples: "Working here has been great" / "Bu yerda ishlash ajoyib bo'ldi"

Infinitives (to + verb): After certain verbs, expressing purpose

O'zbek: Infinitiv shakllar

Examples: "I want to improve my skills" / "Men ko'nikmalarimni yaxshilashni xohlayman"

Phrasal Verbs: Common workplace phrasal verbs

O'zbek: Frasal fe'llar

Examples: "I get up early for work" / "Men ish uchun erta turaman"

7. Prepositions and Location / Predloglar va Joylashuv

Time Prepositions: at, on, in, for, since, during

O'zbek: Vaqt predloglari

Examples: "I work from 8 AM to 5 PM" / "Men ertalab 8 dan kechqurun 5 gacha ishlayman"

Place Prepositions: at, on, in, under, over, between

O'zbek: Joy predloglari

Examples: "The tools are in the shed" / "Asboblar omborxonada"

Movement Prepositions: to, from, into, out of, through

O'zbek: Harakat predloglari

Examples: "I go to work by bus" / "Men ishga avtobusda boraman"

8. Articles and Quantifiers / Artikl va Miqdor Ko'rsatkichlari

Articles: a/an (indefinite), the (definite)

O'zbek: Artikllar

Examples: "I need a job" vs "The job is perfect for me" / "Menga ish kerak" vs "Bu ish men uchun mukammal"

Quantifiers: some, any, much, many, a lot of, a few, a little

O'zbek: Miqdor ko'rsatkichlari

Examples: "I have some experience" / "Menda biroz tajriba bor"
