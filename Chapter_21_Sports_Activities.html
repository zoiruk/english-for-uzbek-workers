Grammar / Grammatika

Bu grammatika nima uchun kerak? (Why do we need this grammar?)

Comparative va Superlative sifatlar sizga narsalarni solishtirish va eng yaxshi yoki eng yomonini aniqlash imkonini beradi. Bu sport va faoliyatlar haqida gaplashayotganda juda muhim. Masalan, "Futbol tennisdan qiziqroq" yoki "Bu eng yaxshi o'yinchi" deyish uchun bu qoidalarni bilish kerak. Hamkasblar bilan sport haqida suhbatlashayotganda bu grammatika juda foydali.

Asosiy tushuncha (Basic Concept)

Comparative (Solishtirma daraja): Ikki narsani solishtirishda ishlatiladi. Masalan: "tezroq", "kuchliroq".

Superlative (Orttirma daraja): Uch yoki undan ko'p narsa ichida eng yaxshisini ko'rsatishda ishlatiladi. Masalan: "eng tez", "eng kuchli".

Qoidalar:

• Qisqa sifatlar (1-2 bo'g'in): -er/-est qo'shiladi

• Uzun sifatlar (3+ bo'g'in): more/most ishlatiladi

• Tartibsiz sifatlar: good-better-best kabi

Amaliy qo'llash (Practical Application)

1. Sport afzalliklarini muhokama qilish (Discussing sport preferences)

Vaziyat: Hamkasblar bilan sport haqida suhbat

Comparative misollar:

• "Football is more exciting than cricket for me." - Men uchun futbol kriketdan hayajonliroq.

• "Swimming is harder than running." - Suzish yugurishdan qiyinroq.

Superlative misollar:

• "Football is the most popular sport here." - Futbol bu yerda eng mashhur sport.

• "Tennis is the most expensive sport to play." - Tennis o'ynash uchun eng qimmat sport.

2. Shaxsiy qobiliyatlarni baholash (Assessing personal abilities)

Vaziyat: O'z sport qobiliyatlari haqida gaplashish

O'zini baholash:

• "I'm better at football than basketball." - Men basketboldan ko'ra futbolda yaxshiroqman.

• "Running is the easiest sport for me." - Yugurish men uchun eng oson sport.

Kamtarlik:

• "I'm not as good as my colleagues." - Men hamkasblarim kabi yaxshi emasman.

• "Swimming is more difficult for me than others." - Suzish men uchun boshqalardan qiyinroq.

3. Sport tadbirlari va natijalar (Sports events and results)

Vaziyat: Sport yangiliklari va natijalar haqida

Natijalarni baholash:

• "This was the best match of the season." - Bu mavsumning eng yaxshi o'yini edi.

• "Our team played better than last week." - Bizning jamoa o'tgan haftadan yaxshiroq o'ynadi.

Prognozlar:

• "The final will be more exciting than today's match." - Final bugungi o'yindan hayajonliroq bo'ladi.

Tez-tez qilinadigan xatolar (Common Mistakes)

❌ Xato (Wrong) ➡️ ✅ To'g'ri (Correct)

1. Noto'g'ri qo'shimcha ishlatish:

❌ "Football is more fast than cricket" ➡️ ✅ "Football is faster than cricket"

❌ "This is the most good player" ➡️ ✅ "This is the best player"

Tushuntirish: Qisqa sifatlarga -er/-est, uzun sifatlarga more/most qo'shiladi.

2. "Than" so'zini unutish:

❌ "Swimming is harder running" ➡️ ✅ "Swimming is harder than running"

❌ "He is stronger his brother" ➡️ ✅ "He is stronger than his brother"

Tushuntirish: Comparative da "than" so'zi majburiy.

3. "The" ni superlative da unutish:

❌ "Football is most popular sport" ➡️ ✅ "Football is the most popular sport"

❌ "She is best player" ➡️ ✅ "She is the best player"

Tushuntirish: Superlative da "the" artikli majburiy.

4. Ikki marta solishtirish:

❌ "This is more better than that" ➡️ ✅ "This is better than that"

❌ "She is more stronger" ➡️ ✅ "She is stronger"

Tushuntirish: Bir vaqtda faqat bitta solishtirish shakli ishlatiladi.
