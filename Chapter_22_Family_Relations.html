Grammar / Grammatika

Bu grammatika nima uchun kerak? (Why do we need this grammar?)

Possessive (egalik) shakllari sizga kimga nima tegishli ekanligini ko'rsatish uchun kerak. Bu oila a'zolari va shaxsiy narsalar haqida gaplashayotganda juda muhim. Masalan, "Mening oilam", "Uning akasi", "Bizning uyimiz" deyish uchun bu qoidalarni bilish shart. Hamkasblar bilan oila haqida suhbatlashayotganda bu grammatika doimo kerak bo'ladi.

Asosiy tushuncha (Basic Concept)

Possessive Adjectives (Egalik sifatlari): Ot oldida kelib, kimga tegishli ekanligini ko'rsatadi. Masalan: my, your, his, her.

Possessive Pronouns (Egalik olmoshlari): Ot o'rnida ishlatiladi. Masalan: mine, yours, his, hers.

Possessive 's: Ism + 's = o'sha kishiga tegishli. Masalan: John's car, Mary's book.

Qoidalar:

• Possessive adjective + ot: "my family"

• Possessive pronoun yakka: "This is mine"

• Ism + 's: "Ahmed's brother"

Amaliy qo'llash (Practical Application)

1. Oila a'zolari haqida gaplashish (Talking about family members)

Vaziyat: Yangi hamkasblar bilan tanishayotganda

O'zingiz haqida:

• "My wife works as a teacher in Uzbekistan." - Mening xotinim O'zbekistonda o'qituvchi bo'lib ishlaydi.

• "Our children are studying at university." - Bizning bolalarimiz universitetda o'qiydi.

Boshqalar haqida so'rash:

• "Tell me about your family." - Menga oilangiz haqida gapirib bering.

• "How many children do you have?" - Sizning nechta farzandingiz bor?

2. Shaxsiy narsalar va tegishlilik (Personal belongings and ownership)

Vaziyat: Umumiy yashash joyida narsalarni ajratish

O'z narsalaringizni ko'rsatish:

• "This is my room." - Bu mening xonam.

• "That phone is mine." - O'sha telefon meniki.

Boshqalarning narsalari:

• "Whose bag is this?" - Bu kimning sumkasi?

• "Is this Ahmed's jacket?" - Bu Ahmedning kurtkami?

3. Oilaviy aloqa va sog'inish (Family contact and missing home)

Vaziyat: Uy-ro'zg'or va oila sog'inchi haqida

Aloqa:

• "I call my parents every Sunday." - Men har yakshanba ota-onamga qo'ng'iroq qilaman.

• "My wife sends me photos of our children." - Xotinim menga bolalarimizning suratlarini yuboradi.

Sog'inish:

• "I miss my family very much." - Men oilamni juda sog'inaman.

• "Our home is very different from here." - Bizning uyimiz bu yerdan juda farq qiladi.

Tez-tez qilinadigan xatolar (Common Mistakes)

❌ Xato (Wrong) ➡️ ✅ To'g'ri (Correct)

1. Possessive adjective va pronoun aralashtirib yuborish:

❌ "This is mine family" ➡️ ✅ "This is my family"

❌ "The book is my" ➡️ ✅ "The book is mine"

Tushuntirish: Ot oldida adjective (my), yakka ishlatganda pronoun (mine).

2. Possessive 's ni noto'g'ri ishlatish:

❌ "Ahmed family" ➡️ ✅ "Ahmed's family"

❌ "My friend's are here" ➡️ ✅ "My friends are here"

Tushuntirish: Egalik uchun 's, ko'plik uchun faqat s.

3. "His" va "her" ni aralashtirib yuborish:

❌ "Maria and his husband" ➡️ ✅ "Maria and her husband"

❌ "Ahmed and her children" ➡️ ✅ "Ahmed and his children"

Tushuntirish: Erkak uchun "his", ayol uchun "her".

4. "Whose" va "Who's" ni aralashtirib yuborish:

❌ "Who's book is this?" ➡️ ✅ "Whose book is this?"

❌ "Whose coming today?" ➡️ ✅ "Who's coming today?"

Tushuntirish: "Whose" = kimniki, "Who's" = who is.
