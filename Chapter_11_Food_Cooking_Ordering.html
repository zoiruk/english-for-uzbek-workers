Grammar / Grammatika

Grammatika - Modal Verbs: Can/Can't, Would Like (Modal Fe'llar)

Bu grammatika nima uchun kerak? (Why do we need this grammar?)

Modal fe'llar ovqat buyurtma berish, iltimos qilish va qobiliyatni ifodalash uchun juda muhim. Mavsumiy ishchilar sifatida siz har kuni ovqat sotib olishingiz, restoranda buyurtma berishingiz va boshqalardan yordam so'rashingiz kerak bo'ladi. "Can" qobiliyat va imkoniyatni, "would like" esa xushmuomalalik bilan xohishni bildiradi. Masalan: "Can I cook here?" yoki "I would like some tea, please".

Asosiy tushuncha (Basic Concept)

Modal fe'llar - bu asosiy fe'l oldidan keladigan yordamchi fe'llar. Ular qobiliyat, imkoniyat, iltimos va xohishni ifodalaydi. Can - qila olish, imkoni bor; Can't - qila olmaslik; Would like - xohlamoq (muloyim shakl). Bu fe'llar bilan asosiy fe'l o'zgarmasdan qoladi: "I can cook", "I would like to eat".

Uch shakl (Three Forms)

1. Can - Qobiliyat va Imkoniyat (Ability and Permission)

Subject can Base Verb Object
I/You/He/She/We/They can cook/eat/order food/dinner

Misollar (Examples):

I can cook simple meals.
[Ай кэн кук симпл милс]
Men oddiy taomlar tayyorlay olaman.
Qobiliyat: Ovqat pishirish mahorati
You can use the kitchen after 6 PM.
[Ю кэн юз зэ кичин афтэр сикс пи эм]
Siz oshxonani soat 6 dan keyin ishlatishingiz mumkin.
Ruxsat: Oshxona qoidalari
We can eat lunch together today.
[Ви кэн ит ланч тугэзэр тудэй]
Bugun birga tushlik qilishimiz mumkin.
Taklif: Birgalik ovqatlanish
She can make traditional Uzbek food.
[Ши кэн мэйк трэдишнл узбэк фуд]
U an'anaviy o'zbek taomlarini tayyorlay oladi.
Mahorat: Milliy taomlar
They can order food online.
[Зэй кэн ордэр фуд онлайн]
Ular onlayn ovqat buyurtma qilishlari mumkin.
Texnologiya: Zamonaviy usul

2. Can't - Qobiliyatsizlik va Taqiq (Inability and Prohibition)

Subject can't (cannot) Base Verb Object
I/You/He/She/We/They can't cook/eat/find spicy food

Misollar (Examples):

I can't eat spicy food.
[Ай кэнт ит спайси фуд]
Men achchiq ovqat yey olmayman.
Cheklov: Ovqat tanlovi
You can't cook in your bedroom.
[Ю кэнт кук ин йор бэдрум]
Yotoq xonangizda ovqat pishira olmaysiz.
Qoida: Turar joy qoidalari
We can't find halal meat in this shop.
[Ви кэнт файнд халал мит ин зис шоп]
Bu do'konda halol go'sht topa olmaymiz.
Muammo: Maxsus talab
She can't drink coffee late at night.
[Ши кэнт дринк кофи лэйт эт найт]
U kechqurun kofe icha olmaydi.
Sog'liq: Shaxsiy cheklov
They can't afford expensive restaurants.
[Зэй кэнт эфорд экспэнсив рэсторантс]
Ular qimmat restoranlarga pul yetkazmaydi.
Moliya: Byudjet cheklovi

3. Would Like - Muloyim Xohish (Polite Requests and Desires)

Subject would like Object/to + verb
I/You/He/She/We/They would like ('d like) some tea / to order

Misollar (Examples):

A: What would you like to drink?
[Вот вуд ю лайк ту дринк?]
Nima ichmoqchisiz?
B: I'd like some tea, please.
[Айд лайк сам ти, плиз]
Choy istardim, iltimos.
Restoran: Ichimlik buyurtmasi
A: Would you like to try our special dish?
[Вуд ю лайк ту трай аур спешал диш?]
Maxsus taomimizni tatib ko'rmoqchimisiz?
B: Yes, I'd like to try it.
[Йес, айд лайк ту трай ит]
Ha, tatib ko'rmoqchiman.
Taklif: Yangi taom sinash
A: Can I help you with anything?
[Кэн ай хэлп ю виз энисинг?]
Biror narsada yordam bera olamanmi?
B: I'd like to order a takeaway, please.
[Айд лайк ту ордэр э тэйкэвэй, плиз]
Olib ketish uchun buyurtma bermoqchiman.
Xizmat: Olib ketish buyurtmasi
A: Would you like some more rice?
[Вуд ю лайк сам мор райс?]
Yana guruch istaysizmi?
B: No, thank you. I'm full.
[Ноу, сэнк ю. Айм фул]
Yo'q, rahmat. To'qdim.
Mehmondo'stlik: Qo'shimcha taklif
A: What would you like for dessert?
[Вот вуд ю лайк фор дэзэрт?]
Shirinlik sifatida nima istaysiz?
B: I'd like some ice cream, please.
[Айд лайк сам айс крим, плиз]
Muzqaymoq istardim, iltimos.
Shirinlik: Dessert tanlovi

Amaliy qo'llash (Practical Application)

Mavsumiy ishchilar uchun muhim vaziyatlar:

  • Restoranda: "I'd like to order fish and chips, please" - ovqat buyurtma berish
  • Oshxonada: "Can I use the microwave?" - ruxsat so'rash
  • Do'konda: "I can't find halal meat" - muammo bildirish
  • Mehmondo'stlik: "Would you like some tea?" - taklif qilish
  • Cheklovlar: "I can't eat pork for religious reasons" - diniy talablar
  • Yordam: "Can you help me cook this dish?" - yordam so'rash

Tez-tez qilinadigan xatolar (Common Mistakes)

❌ Xato: I can to cook
✅ To'g'ri: I can cook
Can dan keyin "to" ishlatilmaydi
❌ Xato: I would like some tea, please?
✅ To'g'ri: I would like some tea, please.
"Would like" gap oxirida so'roq belgisi yo'q
❌ Xato: Can you like some coffee?
✅ To'g'ri: Would you like some coffee?
Taklif uchun "Would you like" ishlatiladi
❌ Xato: I can't to eat spicy food
✅ To'g'ri: I can't eat spicy food
Can't dan keyin ham "to" ishlatilmaydi
❌ Xato: I like would some tea
✅ To'g'ri: I would like some tea
To'g'ri tartib: "would like"

Exercises / Mashqlar

1. Qobiliyat va ruxsat so'rash (Ability and permission requests)
Namuna: _____ I have a sandwich, please? (Can/Could) → Can
_____ I cook in the shared kitchen? (Can/Could)
2. Ovqat buyurtma berish va afzallik bildirish (Food ordering and preference expression)
Namuna: I would like some tea → I'd like some tea
I _____ a cup of coffee, please. (would like/can)
3. Iltimosli so'rov tuzish (Polite request formation)
Namuna: Can you help me? (less polite) → Could you help me? (more polite)
_____ you recommend a good dish? (Could/Can - more polite)
4. Restoran va oshpazlik stsenariylari (Restaurant and cooking scenarios)
Namuna: In a restaurant: May I see the menu?
I _____ eat spicy food. (can/can't - unable to)
5. O'zbek tilidan inglizchaga tarjima qiling (Translate expressing needs and wants)
Namuna: Men choy ichmoqchiman → I would like some tea
Ozgina suv olsam bo'ladimi, iltimos? → _____
6. To'g'ri modal fe'lni tanlang (Choose correct modal verb)
Namuna: I (can/would like) cook pasta → can
_____ you like some dessert? (Can/Would)
7. Oshpazlik ruxsati va qobiliyati (Cooking permission and ability)
Namuna: Can I use the microwave? → Yes, you can
I _____ make traditional Uzbek food. (can/would like - ability)
8. Inkor modal fe'llar (Negative modal verbs)
Namuna: I can eat meat → I can't eat meat (vegetarian)
I _____ any more food, thank you. (wouldn't like/can't)
9. Amaliy ovqat muloqoti (Practical food communication)
Namuna: Waiter: What would you like to drink?
Bu taomda nima borligini aytib bera olasizmi? → _____
10. O'zingizning misolingizni yozing (Write your own example)
