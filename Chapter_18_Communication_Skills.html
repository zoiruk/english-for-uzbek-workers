Grammar / Grammatika

Bu grammatika nima uchun kerak? (Why do we need this grammar?)

Ish joyida va kundalik hayotda siz tez-tez tushunmovchilik bilan duch kelasiz. Ingliz tilini o'rganayotgan paytda bu tabiiy holat. Muhimi - qanday qilib yordam so'rash, aniqlik talab qilish va o'z tushunmasligingizni professional tarzda ifodalashni bilish. Bu ko'nikmalar sizni ishonchli va professional ko'rsatadi.

Asosiy tushuncha (Basic Concept)

Communication Strategies (Muloqot strategiyalari) - bu muloqotda yuzaga keladigan muammolarni hal qilish usullari:

  • Clarification - aniqlik so'rash (tushunmagan narsani qayta so'rash)
  • Confirmation - tasdiqlash (to'g'ri tushunganimni tekshirish)
  • Repetition - takrorlash (qayta aytishni so'rash)
  • Comprehension check - tushunish tekshiruvi (boshqalar tushundimi?)

Uch shakl (Three Forms)

1. Aniqlik so'rash (Asking for Clarification)

Situation Phrase Type Politeness Level
Didn't hear Could you repeat...? Polite
Didn't understand What do you mean by...? Professional
Need slower speech Could you speak slower? Helpful

Misollar (Examples):

Sorry, could you repeat that please?
[Сори, куд ю рипит зэт плиз]
Kechirasiz, buni takrorlay olasizmi?
Eshitmagan paytda
I'm sorry, I don't understand. Could you explain?
[Айм сори, ай донт андэрстэнд. Куд ю иксплэйн]
Kechirasiz, tushunmayapman. Tushuntirib bera olasizmi?
Tushunmagan paytda
What do you mean by "overtime"?
[Вот ду ю мин бай овэртайм]
"Overtime" deganda nimani nazarda tutyapsiz?
So'z ma'nosini so'rash
Could you speak a bit slower, please?
[Куд ю спик э бит слоуэр плиз]
Biroz sekinroq gapira olasizmi?
Tez gapirgan paytda
I didn't catch the last part. Could you say it again?
[Ай дидэнт кэч зэ ласт парт. Куд ю сэй ит эгэйн]
Oxirgi qismini eshitmadim. Yana ayta olasizmi?
Qisman eshitmagan paytda

2. Tushunmaslikni ifodalash (Expressing Non-Understanding)

Problem Type Expression Follow-up
Complete confusion I'm completely lost Could you start again?
Partial understanding I'm not sure I follow Could you clarify?
Technical terms I don't know that word What does it mean?

Misollar (Examples):

I'm sorry, I'm completely lost. Could you start from the beginning?
[Айм сори, айм комплитли лост. Куд ю старт фром зэ бигининг]
Kechirasiz, umuman tushunmayapman. Boshidan boshlaysizmi?
Umuman tushunmagan paytda
I'm not sure I follow. Are you saying that...?
[Айм нот шур ай фоллоу. Ар ю сэйинг зэт]
Aniq tushunmayapman. Siz ... demoqchimisiz?
Qisman tushунgan paytda
I don't know that word. What does "shift pattern" mean?
[Ай донт ноу зэт вёрд. Вот даз шифт пэтэрн мин]
Bu so'zni bilmayman. "Shift pattern" nimani anglatadi?
Yangi so'z uchraganda
I'm having trouble understanding. Could you use simpler words?
[Айм хэвинг трабл андэрстэндинг. Куд ю юз симплэр вёрдс]
Tushunishda qiynalayapman. Oddiyroq so'zlar ishlatа olasizmi?
Murakkab so'zlar ishlatilganda
Sorry, that doesn't make sense to me. Could you explain differently?
[Сори, зэт дазэнт мэйк сэнс ту ми. Куд ю иксплэйн дифэрэнтли]
Kechirasiz, bu menga mantiqiy emas. Boshqacha tushuntirib bera olasizmi?
Mantiqsiz tuyulganda

3. Tushunishni tasdiqlash (Confirming Understanding)

Confirmation Type Structure Purpose
Paraphrasing So you're saying that...? O'z so'zlarim bilan takrorlash
Direct confirmation Do you mean...? To'g'ridan-to'g'ri so'rash
Understanding check Let me check if I understand... Tushunganimni tekshirish

Misollar (Examples):

So you're saying that I need to work on Saturday?
[Соу ёр сэйинг зэт ай нид ту вёрк он сэтэрдэй]
Demak, siz shanba kuni ishlashim kerak deyapsizmi?
O'z so'zlarim bilan takrorlash
Do you mean I should report to the supervisor first?
[Ду ю мин ай шуд рипорт ту зэ супэрвайзэр фёрст]
Avval nazoratchi ga xabar berishim kerak demoqchimisiz?
Aniq ma'noni so'rash
Let me check if I understand correctly. You want me to...?
[Лет ми чек иф ай андэрстэнд корэктли. Ю вонт ми ту]
To'g'ri tushunganimni tekshirib ko'raman. Siz mendan ... ni xohlaysizmi?
Tushunganimni tasdiqlash
Just to confirm - the meeting is at 2 PM, right?
[Джаст ту конфёрм - зэ митинг из эт ту пи эм райт]
Faqat tasdiqlash uchun - yig'ilish 14:00 da, to'g'rimi?
Muhim ma'lumotni tasdiqlash
Can I just double-check? You said the deadline is Friday?
[Кэн ай джаст дабл чек? Ю сэйд зэ дэдлайн из фрайдэй]
Ikki marta tekshirib olsam bo'ladimi? Muddat juma kuni dedingizmi?
Muhim sanani tasdiqlash

Amaliy qo'llash (Practical Application)

Ish ko'rsatmalari (Work instructions):

Clarification: "Sorry, what do you mean by 'quality control'?"

Kechirasiz, "quality control" deganda nimani nazarda tutyapsiz?

Yig'ilishlarda (In meetings):

Confirmation: "So you're saying we need to finish by 5 PM?"

Demak, 17:00 gacha tugatishimiz kerak deyapsizmi?

Telefon suhbatida (On the phone):

Repetition: "Sorry, the line is bad. Could you repeat the address?"

Kechirasiz, aloqa yomon. Manzilni takrorlay olasizmi?

Yangi vazifa (New task):

Understanding check: "Let me make sure I understand the process correctly..."

Jarayonni to'g'ri tushunganimga ishonch hosil qilaman...

Tez-tez qilinadigan xatolar (Common Mistakes)

❌ Xato: "I no understand" (grammatik xato)

✅ To'g'ri: "I don't understand"

Inkor uchun "don't" ishlatish kerak

❌ Xato: "What?" (qo'pol)

✅ To'g'ri: "Sorry, what did you say?" yoki "Pardon?"

Xushmuomala so'rash muhim

❌ Xato: "You speak too fast!" (ayblash)

✅ To'g'ri: "Could you speak a bit slower, please?"

Iltimos shaklida so'rash yaxshiroq
