Grammar / Grammatika

Bu grammatika nima uchun kerak? (Why do we need this grammar?)

Bank ishlarida va moliyaviy rejalashtirishda siz tez-tez kelajak haqida gaplashishingiz kerak bo'ladi. Masalan, pul o'tkazish, kredit olish, jamg'arma rejasi tuzish. Ingliz tilida kelajakni ifodalash uchun ikki asosiy usul bor: "will" va "going to". Har birining o'z vazifasi va ma'nosi bor.

Asosiy tushuncha (Basic Concept)

Future Forms (Kelajak shakllari) - kelajakda sodir bo'ladigan voqealarni ifodalash uchun ishlatiladi:

  • WILL - oniy qaror, bashorat, va'da (spontaneous decision, prediction, promise)
  • GOING TO - oldindan rejalashtirgan ish, aniq belgilar (planned action, evidence-based prediction)

Bank va moliya sohasida ikkalasi ham tez-tez ishlatiladi, lekin har xil vaziyatlarda.

Uch shakl (Three Forms)

1. Tasdiqlovchi shakl (Positive Form)

Future Form Structure Usage Example Context
WILL Subject + will + base verb Oniy qaror, va'da Bank xodimi bilan
GOING TO Subject + am/is/are + going to + base verb Reja, niyat Moliyaviy rejalar

Misollar (Examples):

I will transfer the money tomorrow.
[Ай вил трэнсфэр зэ мани туморроу]
Men ertaga pul o'tkazaman.
Oniy qaror - hozir qaror qildim
I'm going to save £200 every month.
[Айм гоинг ту сэйв ту хандрэд паундс эври манс]
Men har oy 200 funt jamg'araman.
Oldindan rejalashtirgan
The bank will process your application within 5 days.
[Зэ бэнк вил просэс ёр эпликэйшн визин файв дэйс]
Bank sizning arizangizni 5 kun ichida ko'rib chiqadi.
Rasmiy va'da
We're going to buy a house next year.
[Виэр гоинг ту бай э хаус нэкст йиэр]
Biz kelasi yil uy sotib olamiz.
Katta reja - oldindan o'ylangan
I think the interest rates will increase.
[Ай синк зэ интэрэст рэйтс вил инкрис]
Menimcha, foiz stavkalari oshadi.
Bashorat - shaxsiy fikr

2. Inkorlovchi shakl (Negative Form)

Future Form Negative Structure Short Form
WILL NOT Subject + will not + base verb won't
NOT GOING TO Subject + am/is/are + not going to + base verb isn't/aren't going to

Misollar (Examples):

I won't spend all my salary this month.
[Ай воунт спэнд ол май сэлэри зис манс]
Men bu oy butun maoshimni sarflamayman.
Qaror - sarflamaslikka qaror qildim
I'm not going to take a loan right now.
[Айм нот гоинг ту тэйк э лоун райт нау]
Men hozir kredit olmoqchi emasman.
Reja yo'q - oldindan o'ylab qaror qildim
The bank won't approve loans without proper documents.
[Зэ бэнк воунт эпрув лоунс визаут пропэр докюмэнтс]
Bank to'g'ri hujjatlarsiz kreditlarni tasdiqlamaydi.
Qoida - bank siyosati
We're not going to change banks this year.
[Виэр нот гоинг ту чэйндж бэнкс зис йиэр]
Biz bu yil bank almashtirishni rejalashtirmayapmiz.
Reja yo'q - o'ylab qaror qildik
I don't think prices will fall next year.
[Ай донт синк прайсэс вил фол нэкст йиэр]
Menimcha, kelasi yil narxlar tushмaydi.
Salbiy bashorat

3. So'roq shakl (Question Form)

Question Type Structure Purpose
Will questions Will + subject + base verb? Bashorat, va'da so'rash
Going to questions Am/Is/Are + subject + going to + base verb? Reja so'rash

Misollar (Examples):

Will you transfer the money today?
[Вил ю трэнсфэр зэ мани тудэй]
Bugun pul o'tkazasizmi?
Oniy qaror haqida so'rash
Are you going to open a savings account?
[Ар ю гоинг ту оупэн э сэйвингс экаунт]
Jamg'arma hisobi ochmoqchimisiz?
Reja haqida so'rash
Will the bank approve my loan application?
[Вил зэ бэнк эпрув май лоун эпликэйшн]
Bank mening kredit arizamni tasdiqlayдimi?
Bashorat so'rash
What are you going to do with your savings?
[Вот ар ю гоинг ту ду виз ёр сэйвингс]
Jamg'armangiz bilan nima qilmoqchisiz?
Moliyaviy reja so'rash
When will I receive my debit card?
[Вэн вил ай рисив май дэбит кард]
Debit kartamni qachon olaman?
Vaqt haqida so'rash

Amaliy qo'llash (Practical Application)

Bank xodimi bilan (With bank staff):

WILL: "I will provide all the necessary documents."

Men barcha kerakli hujjatlarni taqdim etaman.

Moliyaviy rejalar (Financial planning):

GOING TO: "I'm going to save money for a car."

Men mashina uchun pul jamg'armoqchiman.

Bashoratlar (Predictions):

WILL: "I think the exchange rate will change."

Menimcha, valyuta kursi o'zgaradi.

Aniq belgilar (Clear evidence):

GOING TO: "Look at these bills - I'm going to be broke!"

Bu hisoblarni qarang - men pulsiz qolaman!

Tez-tez qilinadigan xatolar (Common Mistakes)

❌ Xato: "I will going to save money" (ikki kelajak shakli)

✅ To'g'ri: "I will save money" yoki "I'm going to save money"

Faqat bitta kelajak shaklini ishlating

❌ Xato: "I'm going to will transfer" (noto'g'ri tartib)

✅ To'g'ri: "I'm going to transfer" yoki "I will transfer"

Modal fe'llar ketma-ket ishlatilmaydi

❌ Xato: "I go to save money tomorrow" (kelajak shakli yo'q)

✅ To'g'ri: "I'm going to save money tomorrow"

Kelajak uchun kelajak shaklini ishlating
