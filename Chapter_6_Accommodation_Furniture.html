Grammar / Grammatika

Grammatika - "There is/There are" tuzilmalari (There is/There are constructions)

Bu grammatika nima uchun kerak? (Why do we need this grammar?)

"There is/There are" tuzilmalari yordamida siz biror joyda nima borligini yoki yo'qligini aytasiz. Mavsumiy ishchi sifatida bu tuzilmalarni har kuni ishlatasiz - turar joy haqida gapirishda, ish joyini tasvirlashda, muammolarni aytishda va kerakli narsalarni so'rashda.

Misol: "Mening xonamda karavot bor" - "There is a bed in my room"

Asosiy tushuncha (Basic Concept)

"There is/There are" - bu mavjudlik va joylashuvni bildiruvchi maxsus tuzilma. O'zbek tilidagi "bor/yo'q" ma'nosini beradi:

  • There is - bitta narsa yoki sanalmaydigan narsa uchun
  • There are - ko'p narsalar uchun
  • Tuzilish: There + is/are + narsa + joy
  • Qisqa shakl: There's (There is uchun)

Muhim: Bu tuzilmada "there" joy emas, balki grammatik so'z!

Uch shakl (Three Forms)

1. Tasdiqlovchi shakl (Positive Form)

Qachon ishlatamiz: Biror joyda nimadir borligini aytishda

There is/are Narsa Joy O'zbek ma'nosi
There is a bed in my room Xonamda karavot bor
There are two chairs in the kitchen Oshxonada ikkita stul bor
Amaliy misollar (Practical Examples):
There are four beds in our shared room.
[Зэр ар фор бедз ин ауэр шэрд рум]
Bizning umumiy xonamizda to'rtta karavot bor.
Turar joy: Xona tarkibi
There's a washing machine in the basement.
[Зэрз э вошинг машин ин зэ бейсмэнт]
Podvalda kir yuvish mashinasi bor.
Turar joy: Umumiy jihozlar
There are many tools in the farm shed.
[Зэр ар мени тулз ин зэ фарм шед]
Ferma omborida ko'p asboblar bor.
Ish joyi: Asboblar
There's a microwave in the kitchen.
[Зэрз э майкровейв ин зэ кичэн]
Oshxonada mikroto'lqinli pech bor.
Turar joy: Oshxona jihozlari
There are lockers for workers' belongings.
[Зэр ар локэрз фор воркэрз билонгингз]
Ishchilarning buyumlari uchun shkafchalar bor.
Ish joyi: Saqlash joylari

2. Inkorlovchi shakl (Negative Form)

Qachon ishlatamiz: Biror joyda nimadir yo'qligini aytishda

Qanday yasaymiz: There + is/are + not yoki There + is/are + no

Usul 1 Usul 2 Qisqa shakl O'zbek ma'nosi
There is not There is no There isn't Yo'q (birlik)
There are not There are no There aren't Yo'q (ko'plik)
Amaliy misollar (Practical Examples):
There's no hot water in the shower.
[Зэрз ноу хот вотэр ин зэ шауэр]
Dushda issiq suv yo'q.
Muammo: Turar joy shikoyati
There aren't enough chairs for everyone.
[Зэр арнт инаф чэрз фор эвриван]
Hamma uchun etarli stul yo'q.
Muammo: Yetishmaydigan jihozlar
There isn't a fridge in my room.
[Зэр изнт э фридж ин май рум]
Mening xonamda muzlatgich yo'q.
Turar joy: Jihozlar haqida
There are no clean towels in the bathroom.
[Зэр ар ноу клин тауэлз ин зэ басрум]
Hammomda toza sochiqlar yo'q.
Muammo: Tozalik buyumlari
There isn't enough space for all our clothes.
[Зэр изнт инаф спейс фор ол ауэр клоузз]
Barcha kiyimlarimiz uchun etarli joy yo'q.
Turar joy: Joy muammosi

3. So'roq shakl (Question Form)

Qachon ishlatamiz: Biror joyda nimadir bor yoki yo'qligini so'rashda

Qanday yasaymiz: Is/Are + there + narsa + joy?

Is/Are there Narsa Joy O'zbek ma'nosi
Is there a fridge in the kitchen? Oshxonada muzlatgich bormi?
Are there any problems with heating? Isitish bilan muammolar bormi?
Amaliy misollar (Practical Examples):
Is there a washing machine in the building?
[Из зэр э вошинг машин ин зэ билдинг?]
Binoda kir yuvish mashinasi bormi?
Turar joy: Jihozlar haqida
Are there any free beds in the accommodation?
[Ар зэр ени фри бедз ин зэ аккомодейшн?]
Turar joyda bo'sh karavotlar bormi?
Turar joy: Joy qidirish
Is there Wi-Fi in the rooms?
[Из зэр вай-фай ин зэ румз?]
Xonalarda Wi-Fi bormi?
Turar joy: Internet aloqasi
Are there any rules about visitors?
[Ар зэр ени рулз эбаут визиторз?]
Mehmonlar haqida qoidalar bormi?
Turar joy: Qoidalar
How many bathrooms are there in the house?
[Хау мени басрумз ар зэр ин зэ хаус?]
Uyda nechta hammom bor?
Turar joy: Soni haqida

Amaliy qo'llash (Practical Application)

Mavsumiy ishchilar uchun real vaziyatlar:

🏠 Turar joyga birinchi kelish (First arrival at accommodation):

Menejer: "There are four beds in your room and there's a shared kitchen downstairs."

Siz: "Is there a fridge in the kitchen? Are there any rules about cooking times?"

Menejer: "Xonangizda to'rtta karavot bor va pastda umumiy oshxona bor." Siz: "Oshxonada muzlatgich bormi? Ovqat pishirish vaqtlari haqida qoidalar bormi?"

🚿 Muammolarni xabar qilish (Reporting problems):

Siz: "There's no hot water in our bathroom and there are problems with the heating."

Menejer: "I'm sorry. There's a plumber coming tomorrow. Is there anything else?"

Siz: "Bizning hammomda issiq suv yo'q va isitish bilan muammolar bor." Menejer: "Kechirasiz. Ertaga santexnik keladi. Boshqa narsa bormi?"

🔧 Ish joyini o'rganish (Exploring workplace):

Supervayzer: "There are lockers for your belongings and there's a canteen for lunch."

Siz: "Are there showers for workers? Is there a place to dry wet clothes?"

Supervayzer: "Buyumlaringiz uchun shkafchalar bor va tushlik uchun oshxona bor." Siz: "Ishchilar uchun dush bormi? Ho'l kiyimlarni quritish joyi bormi?"

🛏️ Xonadosh bilan gaplashish (Talking with roommate):

Xonadosh: "There isn't much space in the wardrobe. Are there any other storage areas?"

Siz: "There are some boxes under my bed. There's also space in the hallway cupboard."

Xonadosh: "Shkafda ko'p joy yo'q. Boshqa saqlash joylari bormi?" Siz: "Karavotim ostida bir necha quti bor. Koridordagi shkafda ham joy bor."

Tez-tez qilinadigan xatolar (Common Mistakes)

❌ Xato (Wrong):

"There are a bed in my room"

✅ To'g'ri (Correct):

"There is a bed in my room"

Tushuntirish: Bitta narsa uchun "There is" ishlatiladi.

❌ Xato (Wrong):

"There is two chairs"

✅ To'g'ri (Correct):

"There are two chairs"

Tushuntirish: Ko'p narsalar uchun "There are" ishlatiladi.

❌ Xato (Wrong):

"Is there any chairs?"

✅ To'g'ri (Correct):

"Are there any chairs?"

Tushuntirish: Ko'plik bilan "Are there" ishlatiladi.

❌ Xato (Wrong):

"There no is hot water"

✅ To'g'ri (Correct):

"There is no hot water"

Tushuntirish: "There is/are" dan keyin "no" keladi.

❌ Xato (Wrong):

"There it is a problem"

✅ To'g'ri (Correct):

"There is a problem"

Tushuntirish: "There" dan keyin to'g'ridan-to'g'ri "is/are" keladi.

Exercises / Mashqlar

1. Turar joyni tasvirlash (Accommodation description)
Namuna (Example): _____ is a bed → There is a bed

Complete: "_____ _____ a shared kitchen in our accommodation." (birlik - singular)
2. Ish joyidagi ob'ektlar (Workplace facilities)
Namuna (Example): Ko'plik uchun "There are" ishlatamiz

Complete: "_____ _____ three toilets in the farm building." (ko'plik - plural)
3. Joylashuv bo'yicha so'roq (Location-based questions)
Namuna (Example): There is a bed → Is there a bed?

Make a question: "There are washing machines" → "_____ _____ washing machines?"
4. Muammo haqida xabar berish (Problem reporting)
Complete: "_____ _____ a problem with the heating. It doesn't work." (birlik - singular)
5. Yashash sharoitlarini tasvirlash (Describing living conditions)
Complete: "_____ _____ two beds and _____ _____ a wardrobe in my room." (2 beds, 1 wardrobe)
6. Inkor shakli (Negative form)
Namuna (Example): There is → There isn't / There are → There aren't

Make negative: "There are clean towels" → "_____ _____ clean towels."
7. Tarjima: Turar joy (Translation: Accommodation)
Translate: "Bizning xonamizda ikkita karavot bor" → "_____ _____ two beds in our room."
8. Landlord bilan gaplashish (Talking with landlord)
Complete: "Excuse me, _____ _____ no hot water in the shower. Can you fix it?"
9. Yangi kelgan ishchiga yordam (Helping new worker)
Complete: "_____ _____ a shared kitchen downstairs and _____ _____ laundry room upstairs." (1 kitchen, 1 laundry)
10. Amaliy vazifa: Turar joyni to'liq tasvirlash (Practical task: Full accommodation description)
Complete: "In our accommodation, _____ _____ six rooms, _____ _____ one kitchen, and _____ _____ two bathrooms." (6 rooms, 1 kitchen, 2 bathrooms)
