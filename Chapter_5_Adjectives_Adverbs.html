Grammar / Grammatika

Grammatika - Sifatlar va Ravishlar (Adjectives and Adverbs)

Bu grammatika nima uchun kerak? (Why do we need this grammar?)

Sifatlar va ravishlar yordamida siz narsalar va harakatlar haqida batafsil ma'lumot berasiz. Mavsumiy ishchi sifatida bu so'zlarni har kuni ishlatasiz - mahsulot sifatini tasvirlashda, ish sharoitini tushuntirishda, muammolarni aytishda va yordam so'rashda.

Misol: "Bu qulupnaylar juda yangi va pishgan" - "These strawberries are very fresh and ripe"

Asosiy tushuncha (Basic Concept)

Sifatlar (Adjectives) - narsalarning xususiyatlarini tasvirlaydi (qanday?)

Ravishlar (Adverbs) - harakatlarning qanday bajarilishini tasvirlaydi (qanday qilib?)

  • Sifat joylashuvi: Ot oldida (big box) yoki "to be" dan keyin (The box is big)
  • Ravish joylashuvi: Fe'l yonida (work carefully, quickly pick)
  • Ravish yasash: Ko'p sifatlarga -ly qo'shib ravish yasaladi (careful → carefully)
  • Kuchaytirish: very, quite, really, too so'zlari bilan

Uch shakl (Three Forms)

1. Tasdiqlovchi shakl (Positive Form)

Qachon ishlatamiz: Narsalar va harakatlarning ijobiy xususiyatlarini tasvirlashda

Turi Joylashuv Misol O'zbek ma'nosi
Sifat Ot oldida fresh strawberries yangi qulupnaylar
Sifat "to be" dan keyin The box is heavy Quti og'ir
Ravish Fe'l yonida work carefully ehtiyotkorlik bilan ishlash
Amaliy misollar (Practical Examples):
These strawberries are very fresh and ripe.
[Зиз стробериз ар вери фреш энд райп]
Bu qulupnaylar juda yangi va pishgan.
Fermada: Mahsulot sifatini baholash
Please work carefully with sharp tools.
[Плиз ворк кэрфули виз шарп тулз]
Iltimos, o'tkir asboblar bilan ehtiyotkorlik bilan ishlang.
Xavfsizlik: Ish ko'rsatmasi
The weather is really hot today.
[Зэ везэр из рили хот тудей]
Bugun havo haqiqatan issiq.
Ob-havo: Ish sharoiti
She picks fruit quickly and efficiently.
[Ши пикс фрут квикли энд ифишэнтли]
U mevani tez va samarali teradi.
Ish sifati: Baholash
The new gloves fit perfectly.
[Зэ нью главз фит пёрфэктли]
Yangi qo'lqoplar mukammal mos keladi.
Ish kiyimi: Moslik

2. Inkorlovchi shakl (Negative Form)

Qachon ishlatamiz: Narsalar va harakatlarning salbiy xususiyatlarini tasvirlashda

Qanday yasaymiz: "not" qo'shish yoki salbiy sifat/ravish ishlatish

Usul Tuzilish Misol O'zbek ma'nosi
"not" bilan is/are + not + sifat not fresh yangi emas
Salbiy so'z salbiy sifat/ravish old, badly eski, yomon
Amaliy misollar (Practical Examples):
These strawberries are not ripe enough.
[Зиз стробериз ар нот райп инаф]
Bu qulupnaylar etarlicha pishgan emas.
Fermada: Sifatsiz mahsulot
The old tools don't work well.
[Зэ олд тулз донт ворк вел]
Eski asboblar yaxshi ishlamaydi.
Asboblar: Muammo haqida
This box is too heavy for me.
[Зис бокс из ту хэви фор ми]
Bu quti men uchun juda og'ir.
Ish yuklamasi: Yordam so'rash
He doesn't work carefully enough.
[Хи дазнт ворк кэрфули инаф]
U etarlicha ehtiyotkorlik bilan ishlamaydi.
Ish sifati: Tanqid
The accommodation is not comfortable.
[Зэ аккомодейшн из нот камфортэбл]
Turar joy qulay emas.
Turar joy: Shikoyat

3. So'roq shakl (Question Form)

Qachon ishlatamiz: Narsalar va harakatlar haqida ma'lumot olishda

Qanday yasaymiz: "How" + sifat/ravish yoki "Is/Are" + sifat

Savol turi Tuzilish Misol O'zbek ma'nosi
Xususiyat How + sifat + is/are How heavy is it? Qanchalik og'ir?
Harakat How + ravish + do/does How quickly do you work? Qanchalik tez ishlaysiz?
Amaliy misollar (Practical Examples):
How fresh are these strawberries?
[Хау фреш ар зиз стробериз?]
Bu qulupnaylar qanchalik yangi?
Fermada: Sifat tekshiruvi
Is this box too heavy for you?
[Из зис бокс ту хэви фор ю?]
Bu quti siz uchun juda og'irmi?
Yordam: G'amxo'rlik
How carefully should I handle these?
[Хау кэрфули шуд ай хэндл зиз?]
Bularni qanchalik ehtiyotkorlik bilan tutishim kerak?
Ko'rsatma: Xavfsizlik
Are the working conditions comfortable?
[Ар зэ воркинг кондишнз камфортэбл?]
Ish sharoitlari qulayми?
Ish joyi: Baholash
How quickly can we finish this work?
[Хау квикли кэн ви финиш зис ворк?]
Bu ishni qanchalik tez tugata olamiz?
Reja: Vaqt hisoblash

Amaliy qo'llash (Practical Application)

Mavsumiy ishchilar uchun real vaziyatlar:

🍓 Mahsulot sifatini baholash (Assessing product quality):

Supervayzer: "How do these strawberries look? Are they ripe enough?"

Siz: "These are very fresh and perfectly ripe. Those over there are too soft and not good."

Supervayzer: "Bu qulupnaylar qanday ko'rinadi? Etarlicha pishganmi?" Siz: "Bular juda yangi va mukammal pishgan. Ana u yerdagilar juda yumshoq va yaxshi emas."

🏠 Turar joy sharoitlari (Accommodation conditions):

Hamkasb: "How comfortable is your room? Is it big enough?"

Siz: "The room is quite small but clean and comfortable. The bed is really soft and I sleep well."

Hamkasb: "Xonangiz qanchalik qulay? Etarlicha kattami?" Siz: "Xona ancha kichik, lekin toza va qulay. Karavot haqiqatan yumshoq va men yaxshi uxlayman."

⚠️ Xavfsizlik va yordam so'rash (Safety and asking for help):

Siz: "This box is too heavy for me. Can you help me carry it carefully?"

Hamkasb: "Of course! Let's lift it slowly and carefully together."

Siz: "Bu quti men uchun juda og'ir. Uni ehtiyotkorlik bilan ko'tarishda yordam bera olasizmi?" Hamkasb: "Albatta! Keling, uni birga sekin va ehtiyotkorlik bilan ko'taraylik."

🛒 Do'konda xarid qilish (Shopping in store):

Sotuvchi: "How do you like these gloves? Are they comfortable enough?"

Siz: "They fit perfectly and feel very comfortable. How much are they?"

Sotuvchi: "Bu qo'lqoplar sizga qanday yoqadi? Etarlicha qulayми?" Siz: "Ular mukammal mos keladi va juda qulay. Qancha turadi?"

Tez-tez qilinadigan xatolar (Common Mistakes)

❌ Xato (Wrong):

"He works very good"

✅ To'g'ri (Correct):

"He works very well"

Tushuntirish: Fe'l bilan ravish ishlatiladi (well), sifat emas (good).

❌ Xato (Wrong):

"This strawberries are fresh"

✅ To'g'ri (Correct):

"These strawberries are fresh"

Tushuntirish: Ko'plik ot bilan "these" ishlatiladi.

❌ Xato (Wrong):

"The box is heavyly"

✅ To'g'ri (Correct):

"The box is heavy"

Tushuntirish: "To be" dan keyin sifat keladi, ravish emas.

❌ Xato (Wrong):

"She picks careful the fruit"

✅ To'g'ri (Correct):

"She picks the fruit carefully"

Tushuntirish: Fe'l bilan ravish ishlatiladi va ravish odatda oxirda keladi.

❌ Xato (Wrong):

"How much heavy is this?"

✅ To'g'ri (Correct):

"How heavy is this?"

Tushuntirish: Sifat bilan "How" ishlatiladi, "How much" emas.

Exercises / Mashqlar

1. Tasviriy yozish (Descriptive writing)
Namuna (Example): The box is _____ → The box is heavy

Complete: "The strawberries are _____ and _____, perfect for picking." (fresh/ripe)
2. Ish joyida taqqoslash (Workplace comparison)
Namuna (Example): This job is easier than that job

Complete: "This task is _____ than yesterday's task." (easy/hard - choose one)
3. Sifat joylashuvi (Adjective placement)
Namuna (Example): Wrong: "Box heavy the" → Correct: "The heavy box"

Correct this: "Tools sharp these" → "_____ _____ _____"
4. Ravish so'zlar kontekstda (Adverbs in context)
Namuna (Example): He works carefully → U ehtiyotkorlik bilan ishlaydi

Complete: "Please handle the fruit _____. It breaks easily." (carefully/quickly)
5. Muammolarni tasvirlash (Describing problems)
Complete: "The machine is _____ and the tools are _____. We need help." (broken/dirty)
6. Ob-havo va sharoitlar (Weather and conditions)
Namuna (Example): Today is very hot → Bugun juda issiq

Complete: "The weather is _____ hot. We need to drink water _____." (really/often)
7. Tarjima: Sifat va ravish so'zlar (Translation: Adjectives and adverbs)
Translate: "U tez va ehtiyotkorlik bilan ishlaydi" → "He works _____ and _____."
8. Yetarlilik ifodasi (Expressing sufficiency)
Namuna (Example): The box is big enough → Quti yetarlicha katta

Complete: "This ladder is not _____ _____. We need a longer one." (tall enough)
9. Qarama-qarshi ma'nolar (Opposites)
Complete: "Yesterday the work was _____, but today it's _____." (easy/hard)
10. Amaliy vazifa: Ish holatini tasvirlash (Practical task: Describe work situation)
Complete: "The supervisor is _____ happy because we work _____ and finish _____." (very/well/quickly)
