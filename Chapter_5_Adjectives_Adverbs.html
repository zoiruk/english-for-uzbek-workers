Grammar / Grammatika

Grammatika - Sifatlar va Ravishlar (Adjectives and Adverbs)

Bu grammatika nima uchun kerak? (Why do we need this grammar?)

Sifatlar va ravishlar yordamida siz narsalar va harakatlar haqida batafsil ma'lumot berasiz. Mavsumiy ishchi sifatida bu so'zlarni har kuni ishlatasiz - mahsulot sifatini tasvirlashda, ish sharoitini tushuntirishda, muammolarni aytishda va yordam so'rashda.

Misol: "Bu qulupnaylar juda yangi va pishgan" - "These strawberries are very fresh and ripe"

Asosiy tushuncha (Basic Concept)

Sifatlar (Adjectives) - narsalarning xususiyatlarini tasvirlaydi (qanday?)

Ravishlar (Adverbs) - harakatlarning qanday bajarilishini tasvirlaydi (qanday qilib?)

  • Sifat joylashuvi: Ot oldida (big box) yoki "to be" dan keyin (The box is big)
  • Ravish joylashuvi: Fe'l yonida (work carefully, quickly pick)
  • Ravish yasash: Ko'p sifatlarga -ly qo'shib ravish yasaladi (careful ‚Üí carefully)
  • Kuchaytirish: very, quite, really, too so'zlari bilan

Exercises / Mashqlar

1. Tasviriy yozish (Descriptive writing)
Namuna (Example): The box is _____ ‚Üí The box is heavy

Complete: "The strawberries are _____ and _____, perfect for picking." (fresh/ripe)
2. Ish joyida taqqoslash (Workplace comparison)
Namuna (Example): This job is easier than that job

Complete: "This task is _____ than yesterday's task." (easy/hard - choose one)
3. Sifat joylashuvi (Adjective placement)
Namuna (Example): Wrong: "Box heavy the" ‚Üí Correct: "The heavy box"

Correct this: "Tools sharp these" ‚Üí "_____ _____ _____"
4. Ravish so'zlar kontekstda (Adverbs in context)
Namuna (Example): He works carefully ‚Üí U ehtiyotkorlik bilan ishlaydi

Complete: "Please handle the fruit _____. It breaks easily." (carefully/quickly)
5. Muammolarni tasvirlash (Describing problems)
Complete: "The machine is _____ and the tools are _____. We need help." (broken/dirty)
6. Ob-havo va sharoitlar (Weather and conditions)
Namuna (Example): Today is very hot ‚Üí Bugun juda issiq

Complete: "The weather is _____ hot. We need to drink water _____." (really/often)
7. Tarjima: Sifat va ravish so'zlar (Translation: Adjectives and adverbs)
Translate: "U tez va ehtiyotkorlik bilan ishlaydi" ‚Üí "He works _____ and _____."
8. Yetarlilik ifodasi (Expressing sufficiency)
Namuna (Example): The box is big enough ‚Üí Quti yetarlicha katta

Complete: "This ladder is not _____ _____. We need a longer one." (tall enough)
9. Qarama-qarshi ma'nolar (Opposites)
Complete: "Yesterday the work was _____, but today it's _____." (easy/hard)
10. Amaliy vazifa: Ish holatini tasvirlash (Practical task: Describe work situation)
Complete: "The supervisor is _____ happy because we work _____ and finish _____." (very/well/quickly)
