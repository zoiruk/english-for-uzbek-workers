Grammar / Grammatika

Grammatika - Countable/Uncountable Nouns and Quantifiers (Sanaladigan/Sanalmaydigan Otlar va Miqdor Ko'rsatkichlari)

Bu grammatika nima uchun kerak? (Why do we need this grammar?)

Xarid qilishda siz har xil mahsulotlar haqida gapirishingiz kerak bo'ladi. Ba'zi narsalarni sanash mumkin (apples, bananas), ba'zilarini esa sanab bo'lmaydi (milk, bread). Har bir tur uchun turli miqdor so'zlari ishlatiladi. Masalan: "I need some milk" yoki "I want three apples". Bu grammatikani bilmasangiz, do'konda to'g'ri xarid qila olmaysiz.

Asosiy tushuncha (Basic Concept)

Ingliz tilida otlar ikki turga bo'linadi: Countable nouns (sanaladigan otlar) - ularni sanash mumkin va ko'plik shakli bor (apple → apples). Uncountable nouns (sanalmaydigan otlar) - ularni sanab bo'lmaydi va ko'plik shakli yo'q (milk, water, bread). Har bir tur bilan turli miqdor so'zlari ishlatiladi: some, any, much, many, a lot of.

Uch shakl (Three Forms)

1. Sanaladigan otlar (Countable Nouns)

Quantifier Usage Example
a/an birlik uchun a banana, an apple
some tasdiqlovchi gaplarda some apples
any so'roq va inkor gaplarda any bananas?
many ko'p miqdor many oranges
a few oz miqdor a few tomatoes
numbers aniq miqdor three eggs

Misollar (Examples):

I need some apples for lunch.
[Ай нид сам эплс фор ланч]
Tushlik uchun olma kerak.
Xarid: Meva sotib olish
Do you have any bananas?
[Ду ю хэв эни бананас?]
Bananingiz bormi?
So'rash: Mahsulot mavjudligi
I bought three packets of biscuits.
[Ай бот сри пэкитс ов бискитс]
Uch paket pechene sotib oldim.
Miqdor: Aniq son aytish
There are many vegetables in this shop.
[Зэр ар мэни веджитэблс ин зис шоп]
Bu do'konda ko'p sabzavot bor.
Tavsif: Do'kon haqida ma'lumot
Can I have a few carrots, please?
[Кэн ай хэв э фю кэротс, плиз?]
Bir nechta sabzi olsam bo'ladimi?
Iltimos: Oz miqdor so'rash

2. Sanalmaydigan otlar (Uncountable Nouns)

Quantifier Usage Example
some tasdiqlovchi gaplarda some milk
any so'roq va inkor gaplarda any bread?
much ko'p miqdor much water
a little oz miqdor a little sugar
a lot of ko'p (ikkalasi uchun) a lot of rice

Misollar (Examples):

I need some milk for my tea.
[Ай нид сам милк фор май ти]
Choyim uchun sut kerak.
Ichimlik: Sut sotib olish
Is there any bread left?
[Из зэр эни брэд лефт?]
Non qoldimi?
Tekshirish: Mahsulot qolgan-qolmagani
There isn't much sugar in this shop.
[Зэр изнт мач шугар ин зис шоп]
Bu do'konda shakar ko'p emas.
Kamchilik: Mahsulot yetishmasligi
Can I have a little cheese, please?
[Кэн ай хэв э литл чиз, плиз?]
Ozgina pishloq olsam bo'ladimi?
So'rash: Oz miqdor talab qilish
I bought a lot of rice for the week.
[Ай бот э лот ов райс фор зэ вик]
Hafta uchun ko'p guruch sotib oldim.
Rejalashtirish: Haftalik xarid

3. Miqdor haqida so'rash (Asking about Quantities)

Question Word Used With Example
How many Countable nouns How many apples?
How much Uncountable nouns How much milk?
How much Price (narx) How much is it?

Misollar (Examples):

A: How many eggs do you need?
[Хау мэни эгс ду ю нид?]
Nechta tuxum kerak?
B: I need six eggs for the cake.
[Ай нид сикс эгс фор зэ кэйк]
Tort uchun oltita tuxum kerak.
Ovqat tayyorlash: Ingredientlar
A: How much bread should I buy?
[Хау мач брэд шуд ай бай?]
Qancha non sotib olishim kerak?
B: Just one loaf is enough.
[Джаст ван лоуф из инаф]
Faqat bitta non yetarli.
Maslahat: Miqdor tavsiyasi
A: How much does this chicken cost?
[Хау мач даз зис чикин кост?]
Bu tovuq qancha turadi?
B: It's £8.50 per kilogram.
[Итс эйт паундс фифти пэр килограм]
Kilogrammi 8 funt 50 pens.
Narx: Go'sht narxini so'rash
A: Do you have any special offers today?
[Ду ю хэв эни спешал офэрс тудэй?]
Bugun maxsus takliflaringiz bormi?
B: Yes, buy two, get one free on all fruits.
[Йес, бай ту, гет ван фри он ол фрутс]
Ha, barcha mevalar uchun ikkitasini oling, bittasi bepul.
Chegirma: Maxsus takliflar
A: Is there any discount for buying in bulk?
[Из зэр эни дискаунт фор байинг ин балк?]
Ko'p miqdorda sotib olish uchun chegirma bormi?
B: Yes, 10% off for orders over £50.
[Йес, тен пэрсент оф фор ордэрс оувэр фифти паундс]
Ha, 50 funtdan ortiq xarid uchun 10% chegirma.
Tejash: Katta xarid

Amaliy qo'llash (Practical Application)

Mavsumiy ishchilar uchun muhim vaziyatlar:

  • Haftalik xarid: "I need some rice, a few vegetables, and much water" - asosiy oziq-ovqat
  • Narx so'rash: "How much is this bread?" - mahsulot narxini bilish
  • Miqdor so'rash: "How many apples are in this bag?" - paket tarkibi
  • Chegirma: "Do you have any special offers?" - tejash imkoniyatlari
  • Yetarlilik: "Is there enough milk for the week?" - haftalik ehtiyoj
  • Taqqoslash: "This shop has many vegetables but not much meat" - do'konlarni solishtirish

Tez-tez qilinadigan xatolar (Common Mistakes)

❌ Xato: How many milk do you need?
✅ To'g'ri: How much milk do you need?
Milk sanalmaydigan ot, shuning uchun "how much" ishlatiladi
❌ Xato: I need much apples
✅ To'g'ri: I need many apples
Apples sanaladigan ot, shuning uchun "many" ishlatiladi
❌ Xato: I have some breads
✅ To'g'ri: I have some bread
Bread sanalmaydigan ot, ko'plik shakli yo'q
❌ Xato: Do you have any apple?
✅ To'g'ri: Do you have any apples?
"Any" bilan sanaladigan otlar ko'plik shaklida ishlatiladi
❌ Xato: I bought a rice
✅ To'g'ri: I bought some rice
Rice sanalmaydigan ot, "a/an" ishlatilmaydi

Exercises / Mashqlar

1. Xarid ro'yxati va miqdor (Shopping list and quantity)
Namuna: I need _____ bread for the week. (some/any) → some
I need _____ milk for breakfast. (some/any)
2. Sanaladigan va sanalmaydigan otlarni ajrating (Classify countable/uncountable nouns)
Namuna: Money - uncountable (much/little)
How _____ apples do you want? (much/many)
3. Narx va miqdor bo'yicha muzokaralar (Price and amount negotiation)
Namuna: How much does this cost? → £5.50
_____ does the bread cost? (How much/How many)
4. Tranzaksiyani yakunlash stsenariylari (Transaction completion scenarios)
Namuna: Do you have any change? → Yes, here's your change
Do you have _____ plastic bags? (some/any)
5. O'zbek tilidan inglizchaga tarjima qiling (Translate shopping interactions)
Namuna: Menga ozgina non kerak → I need some bread
Menda hech qanday pul yo'q → _____
6. To'g'ri miqdor ko'rsatkichni tanlang (Choose correct quantifier)
Namuna: There are (a lot of/much) people in the shop → a lot of
I don't have _____ time for shopping. (many/much)
7. Xarid miqdor ifodalari (Shopping quantity expressions)
Namuna: A bottle of water, a loaf of bread
I need _____ bananas for lunch. (a few/a little)
8. Inkor miqdor ko'rsatkichlari (Negative quantifier practice)
Namuna: There isn't any milk left → We need to buy some
_____ customers in the shop today. (There isn't much/There aren't many)
9. Amaliy xarid muloqoti (Practical shopping communication)
Namuna: Excuse me, do you have any discount items?
Sabzavotlarga maxsus taklif bormi? → _____
10. O'zingizning misolingizni yozing (Write your own example)
Namuna: I need some bread, a few apples, and a little cheese for dinner
Haftalik xarid ro'yxatini miqdor ko'rsatkichlari bilan yozing (Write a weekly shopping list using quantifiers)
