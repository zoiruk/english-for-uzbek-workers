Grammar / Grammatika

Grammatika - Ko'rsatish olmoshlari (this/that/these/those)

Bu grammatika nima uchun kerak? (Why do we need this grammar?)

Ko'rsatish olmoshlari yordamida siz narsalarni ko'rsatasiz va ular haqida gapirasiz. Mavsumiy ishchi sifatida bu so'zlarni har kuni ishlatasiz - asboblarni ko'rsatishda, ranglarni aytishda, do'konda xarid qilishda va ish joyida narsalarni topishda.

Misol: "Bu qizil chelak qulupnay uchun" - "This red bucket is for strawberries"

Asosiy tushuncha (Basic Concept)

Ko'rsatish olmoshlari narsalarning joylashuvi (yaqin yoki uzoq) va soni (bitta yoki ko'p) bo'yicha farqlanadi:

  • This - yaqin joylashgan bitta narsa uchun
  • That - uzoq joylashgan bitta narsa uchun
  • These - yaqin joylashgan ko'p narsalar uchun
  • Those - uzoq joylashgan ko'p narsalar uchun

Masofa qoidasi: Qo'lingiz bilan yetib olsangiz - "this/these", yeta olmasangiz - "that/those"

Uch shakl (Three Forms)

1. Tasdiqlovchi shakl (Positive Form)

Qachon ishlatamiz: Narsalarni ko'rsatish va ular haqida ma'lumot berishda

Ko'rsatish Masofa Son O'zbek ma'nosi
This Yaqin Birlik Bu
That Uzoq Birlik U/O'sha
These Yaqin Ko'plik Bular
Those Uzoq Ko'plik Ular/O'shalar
Amaliy misollar (Practical Examples):
This is my red bucket.
[Зис из май ред бакет]
Bu mening qizil chelakim.
Fermada: Yaqindagi asbobni ko'rsatish
That is the supervisor's office.
[Зэт из зэ супервайзерз офис]
U supervayzer ofisi.
Fermada: Uzoqdagi binoni ko'rsatish
These are my work gloves.
[Зиз ар май ворк главз]
Bular mening ish qo'lqoplarim.
Ish kiyimi: Yaqindagi narsalarni ko'rsatish
Those are strawberry plants.
[Зоуз ар стробери плэнтс]
Ular qulupnay o'simliklari.
Fermada: Uzoqdagi o'simliklarni ko'rsatish
This knife is very sharp.
[Зис найф из вери шарп]
Bu pichoq juda o'tkir.
Xavfsizlik: Asbob haqida ogohlantirish

2. Inkorlovchi shakl (Negative Form)

Qachon ishlatamiz: Narsalar haqida noto'g'ri ma'lumotni rad etishda

Qanday yasaymiz: Ko'rsatish olmoshi + "is not" yoki "are not"

Ko'rsatish To Be + Not Qisqa shakl O'zbek ma'nosi
This/That is not isn't Bu/U ... emas
These/Those are not aren't Bular/Ular ... emas
Amaliy misollar (Practical Examples):
This is not my bag.
[Зис из нот май бэг]
Bu mening sumkam emas.
Turar joyda: Noto'g'ri ma'lumotni tuzatish
That isn't the right tool.
[Зэт изнт зэ райт тул]
U to'g'ri asbob emas.
Fermada: Noto'g'ri asbobni rad etish
These aren't ripe strawberries.
[Зиз арнт райп стробериз]
Bular pishgan qulupnaylar emas.
Fermada: Mahsulot sifatini baholash
Those are not our boxes.
[Зоуз ар нот ауэр боксиз]
Ular bizning qutilarimiz emas.
Ish joyida: Mulkni ajratish
This isn't the right colour.
[Зис изнт зэ райт калэр]
Bu to'g'ri rang emas.
Do'konda: Xarid qilishda

3. So'roq shakl (Question Form)

Qachon ishlatamiz: Narsalar haqida savol berishda va ma'lumot olishda

Qanday yasaymiz: "Is" yoki "Are" ni gapning boshiga qo'yamiz

Is/Are Ko'rsatish Ma'lumot O'zbek ma'nosi
Is this/that your bag? Bu/U sizning sumkangizmi?
Are these/those ready? Bular/Ular tayyormi?
Amaliy misollar (Practical Examples):
What colour is this bucket?
[Вот калэр из зис бакет?]
Bu chelak qanday rang?
Fermada: Asbob haqida savol
Is that your supervisor?
[Из зэт ёр супервайзер?]
U sizning supervayzeringizmi?
Ish joyida: Odamlarni aniqlash
Are these strawberries ripe?
[Ар зиз стробериз райп?]
Bu qulupnaylar pishganmi?
Fermada: Mahsulot sifatini tekshirish
Are those the right tools?
[Ар зоуз зэ райт тулз?]
Ular to'g'ri asboblarmi?
Ish joyida: Asboblarni tasdiqlash
What are those plants called?
[Вот ар зоуз плэнтс колд?]
O'sha o'simliklar nima deb ataladi?
Fermada: Yangi o'simliklar haqida

Amaliy qo'llash (Practical Application)

Mavsumiy ishchilar uchun real vaziyatlar:

🧰 Fermada asboblar bilan ishlash (Working with tools on farm):

Supervayzer: "Use this red bucket for strawberries and that blue one for raspberries."

Siz: "Is this bucket clean? Those buckets over there look dirty."

Supervayzer: "Bu qizil chelakni qulupnay uchun, o'sha ko'k chelakni malina uchun ishlating." Siz: "Bu chelak tozami? Ana u yerdagi chelaklar iflos ko'rinadi."

🛒 Do'konda xarid qilish (Shopping in store):

Sotuvchi: "What colour gloves do you need?"

Siz: "I need these blue gloves. How much are those yellow ones?"

Sotuvchi: "Qanday rangli qo'lqop kerak?" Siz: "Menga bu ko'k qo'lqoplar kerak. O'sha sariq qo'lqoplar qancha turadi?"

🏠 Turar joyda narsalarni topish (Finding things at accommodation):

Xonadosh: "Is this your phone charger?"

Siz: "No, that's not mine. My charger is black, this one is white."

Xonadosh: "Bu sizning telefon zaryadlovchingizmi?" Siz: "Yo'q, u meniki emas. Mening zaryadlovchim qora, bu esa oq."

🌱 Fermada o'simliklar bilan ishlash (Working with plants on farm):

Tajribali ishchi: "These plants are ready to harvest, but those aren't."

Siz: "What about this row? Are these strawberries ripe enough?"

Tajribali ishchi: "Bu o'simliklar yig'ishga tayyor, lekin ular yo'q." Siz: "Bu qator-chi? Bu qulupnaylar etarlicha pishganmi?"

Tez-tez qilinadigan xatolar (Common Mistakes)

❌ Xato (Wrong):

"These is my gloves"

✅ To'g'ri (Correct):

"These are my gloves"

Tushuntirish: "These/Those" bilan doimo "are" ishlatiladi.

❌ Xato (Wrong):

"That are my tools"

✅ To'g'ri (Correct):

"Those are my tools"

Tushuntirish: Ko'p narsalar uchun "those" ishlatiladi, "that" emas.

❌ Xato (Wrong):

"This are red buckets"

✅ To'g'ri (Correct):

"These are red buckets"

Tushuntirish: Ko'p narsalar uchun "these" ishlatiladi, "this" emas.

❌ Xato (Wrong):

"Those is not mine"

✅ To'g'ri (Correct):

"Those are not mine"

Tushuntirish: "Those" bilan "are" ishlatiladi, "is" emas.

❌ Xato (Wrong):

"What colour are this?"

✅ To'g'ri (Correct):

"What colour is this?"

Tushuntirish: "This/That" bilan "is" ishlatiladi, "are" emas.

Exercises / Mashqlar

1. Narsalarni ko'rsatish (Object identification)
Namuna (Example): _____ is a red bucket (yaqin) → This is a red bucket

Complete: "_____ gloves are dirty." (yaqin, ko'plik - close, plural)
2. Masofa bo'yicha tanlash (Distance-based selection)
Namuna (Example): Yaqin narsa uchun "this", uzoq narsa uchun "that"

Choose: "_____ tools over there are expensive." (uzoq, ko'plik - far, plural)
3. Do'konda: Tarjima qiling (Shopping: Translate)
Namuna (Example): Bu qizil chelak → This is a red bucket

Translate: "O'sha ko'k qo'lqoplar qancha turadi?" → "How much are _____ blue gloves?"
4. Ferma asboblari: Bo'sh joyni to'ldiring (Farm equipment: Fill in the blanks)
Complete: "Can you pass me _____ hammer?" (yaqin - close to you)
5. Ranglar bilan mashq (Colors practice)
Complete: "_____ green boxes are heavy, but _____ red box is light." (uzoq ko'plik, yaqin birlik)
6. Joyni tasvirlash (Describing locations)
Namuna (Example): Bu yerda → here, O'sha yerda → there

Complete: "_____ tools here are mine, _____ tools there are yours."
7. Do'konda so'rash (Shopping scenario)
Translate: "Bu pichoq o'tkir" → "_____ knife is sharp."
8. Ishda: Asboblarni so'rash (At work: Asking for tools)
Complete: "I need _____ screwdriver and _____ nails." (yaqin birlik, uzoq ko'plik)
9. Rang va narsa (Color and object)
Complete: "_____ purple bucket is full, but _____ yellow buckets are empty." (yaqin birlik, uzoq ko'plik)
10. Amaliy vazifa: Supervayzer bilan (Practical task: With supervisor)
Complete: "Supervisor, _____ tools are broken. Can we use _____ new tools instead?" (yaqin ko'plik, uzoq ko'plik)
