Grammar / Grammatika

Bu grammatika nima uchun kerak? (Why do we need this grammar?)

Ish joyida supervayzer va ish beruvchi sizga turli xil majburiyatlar va maslahatlar beradi. Ba'zi narsalar juda muhim (must), ba'zilari tavsiya (should), ba'zilari esa qonuniy majburiyat (have to). Bu modal fe'llarni bilish sizga ish joyidagi qoidalar va talablarni to'g'ri tushunishga yordam beradi.

Asosiy tushuncha (Basic Concept)

Modal verbs (Yordamchi fe'llar) - bu asosiy fe'l bilan birgalikda ishlatiluvchi maxsus fe'llar. Ular majburiyat, zarurat va maslahat darajasini ko'rsatadi:

  • MUST - eng kuchli majburiyat (100% kerak)
  • HAVE TO - tashqi majburiyat (qonun, qoida)
  • SHOULD - maslahat, tavsiya (yaxshi bo'ladi)

Uch shakl (Three Forms)

1. Tasdiqlovchi shakl (Positive Form)

Modal Verb Structure Meaning Strength
MUST Subject + must + base verb Majburiy 100%
HAVE TO Subject + have to + base verb Qoida bo'yicha 95%
SHOULD Subject + should + base verb Tavsiya 70%

Misollar (Examples):

You must wear safety equipment at all times.
[Ю маст вэр сэйфти иквипмэнт эт ол таймс]
Siz doimo xavfsizlik jihozlarini kiyishingiz shart.
Majburiy qoida - xavfsizlik uchun
All workers have to complete safety training.
[Ол воркэрс хэв ту комплит сэйфти трэйнинг]
Barcha ishchilar xavfsizlik o'qitishini tugatishlari kerak.
Kompaniya qoidasi
You should arrive 10 minutes early.
[Ю шуд эрайв тэн минитс ёрли]
Siz 10 daqiqa erta kelishingiz kerak.
Yaxshi maslahat
Employees must report accidents immediately.
[Эмплойис маст рипорт эксидэнтс имидиэтли]
Xodimlar baxtsiz hodisalar haqida darhol xabar berishlari shart.
Jiddiy majburiyat
I have to work overtime this week.
[Ай хэв ту вёрк овэртайм зис вик]
Men bu hafta qo'shimcha ishlashim kerak.
Tashqi majburiyat

2. Inkorlovchi shakl (Negative Form)

Modal Verb Negative Form Meaning
MUST NOT must not / mustn't Taqiqlangan
DON'T HAVE TO don't have to Majburiy emas
SHOULDN'T should not / shouldn't Tavsiya qilinmaydi

Misollar (Examples):

You must not smoke in the workplace.
[Ю маст нот смоук ин зэ вёркплэйс]
Ish joyida chekish taqiqlangan.
Qat'iy taqiq
You don't have to work on Sundays.
[Ю донт хэв ту вёрк он сандэйс]
Yakshanba kunlari ishlash majburiy emas.
Ixtiyoriy
You shouldn't be late for meetings.
[Ю шудэнт би лэйт фор митингс]
Yig'ilishlarga kech qolmasligingiz kerak.
Yomon tavsiya
Workers mustn't operate machinery without training.
[Воркэрс масэнт опэрэйт мэшинэри визаут трэйнинг]
Ishchilar o'qitishsiz mexanizmlarni ishlatishlari mumkin emas.
Xavfsizlik taqiqi
You don't have to bring your own tools.
[Ю донт хэв ту бринг ёр оун тулс]
O'z asboblaringizni olib kelishingiz shart emas.
Kompaniya ta'minlaydi

3. So'roq shakl (Question Form)

Question Type Structure Purpose
Must I...? Must + subject + base verb? Majburiyat so'rash
Do I have to...? Do + subject + have to + base verb? Qoida so'rash
Should I...? Should + subject + base verb? Maslahat so'rash

Misollar (Examples):

Must I wear a helmet in this area?
[Маст ай вэр э хэлмит ин зис эриэ]
Bu hududda dubulg'a kiyishim shartmi?
Majburiyat haqida so'rash
Do I have to work overtime today?
[Ду ай хэв ту вёрк овэртайм тудэй]
Bugun qo'shimcha ishlashim kerakmi?
Qoida haqida so'rash
Should I report this to my supervisor?
[Шуд ай рипорт зис ту май супэрвайзэр]
Buni nazoratchi ga xabar berishim kerakmi?
Maslahat so'rash
Do we have to attend the safety meeting?
[Ду ви хэв ту этэнд зэ сэйфти митинг]
Xavfsizlik yig'ilishida qatnashishimiz kerakmi?
Majburiyat aniqlashtirish
Should we finish this task today?
[Шуд ви финиш зис таск тудэй]
Bu vazifani bugun tugatishimiz kerakmi?
Reja haqida so'rash

Amaliy qo'llash (Practical Application)

Xavfsizlik qoidalari (Safety rules):

MUST: "All employees must wear protective equipment."

Barcha xodimlar himoya vositalarini kiyishlari shart.

Kompaniya qoidalari (Company rules):

HAVE TO: "Workers have to clock in before 7 AM."

Ishchilar ertalab 7 gacha vaqtni belgilashlari kerak.

Maslahatlar (Advice):

SHOULD: "You should take regular breaks to avoid fatigue."

Charchoqni oldini olish uchun muntazam tanaffus qilishingiz kerak.

Taqiqlar (Prohibitions):

MUST NOT: "You must not use your phone during work hours."

Ish vaqtida telefoningizni ishlatish taqiqlangan.

Tez-tez qilinadigan xatolar (Common Mistakes)

❌ Xato: "I must to wear gloves" (ortiqcha "to")

✅ To'g'ri: "I must wear gloves"

Modal fe'llardan keyin "to" ishlatilmaydi

❌ Xato: "I don't must work" (noto'g'ri inkor)

✅ To'g'ri: "I mustn't work" yoki "I don't have to work"

Must ning inkori "mustn't" (taqiq) yoki "don't have to" (majburiy emas)

❌ Xato: "Do I must come early?" (noto'g'ri so'roq)

✅ To'g'ri: "Must I come early?"

Must bilan so'roq qilganda "do" ishlatilmaydi

Examples / Misollar

Could you please repeat the instructions? I want to make sure I understand.
Iltimos, ko'rsatmalarni takrorlay olasizmi? To'g'ri tushunganimga ishonch hosil qilmoqchiman.
Let me confirm: you want me to start at 7 AM in greenhouse 2, correct?
Tasdiqlashga ijozat bering: siz mendan ertalab 7 da 2-issiqxonada boshlashimni xohlaysiz, to'g'rimi?
What time should I take my lunch break today?
Bugun soat nechada tushlik tanaffusini qilishim kerak?
How long will this task take to complete?
Bu vazifani bajarish qancha vaqt oladi?
Who should I report to if there are any problems?
Agar biron-bir muammo bo'lsa, kimga hisobot berishim kerak?
I understand the priority is to finish the strawberry picking first.
Men tushundim, ustuvorlik avval qulupnay terishni tugatishdir.
Thank you for explaining the new procedure. I'll follow it exactly.
Yangi tartib-taomilni tushuntirganing uchun rahmat. Men uni aniq bajaraman.
I'll make sure to update you on my progress every two hours.
Men har ikki soatda muvaffaqiyatim haqida sizga xabar berishni ta'minlayman.
Could you clarify what the quality standards are for this product?
Bu mahsulot uchun sifat me'yorlari qanday ekanligini aniqlashtira olasizmi?
I'll get started right away and let you know when it's finished.
Men darhol boshlayaman va tugagach sizga xabar beraman.

Dialogues / Suhbatlar

Supervisor:
Good morning, Ahmed. Today I need you to work in the packing shed.
Xayrli tong, Ahmad. Bugun sizga qadoqlash omborida ishlash kerak.
Worker:
Could you please explain what exactly I need to do there?
Iltimos, u yerda aynan nima qilishim kerakligini tushuntirib bera olasizmi?
Supervisor:
You'll be sorting and packing tomatoes. The quality standards are posted on the wall.
Siz pomidorlarni saralab, qadoqlaysiz. Sifat me'yorlari devorda yozilgan.
Worker:
Let me confirm: I sort by size and quality, then pack them in the export boxes?
Tasdiqlashga ijozat bering: men o'lcham va sifat bo'yicha saralab, keyin eksport qutilariga qadoqlayman?
Worker:
What time should I take my break today?
Bugun soat nechada tanaffus qilishim kerak?
Supervisor:
Take your break at 10:30 AM, and lunch break at 12:30 PM.
Ertalab 10:30 da tanaffus qiling va 12:30 da tushlik tanaffusi qiling.
Supervisor:
We need to finish this section by 3 PM. Can you work a bit faster?
Bu bo'limni 15:00 ga tugatishimiz kerak. Biroz tezroq ishlay olasizmi?
Worker:
I understand. I'll increase my pace while maintaining the quality standards.
Tushundim. Men sifat me'yorlarini saqlab, tezligimni oshiraman.
Worker:
Could you repeat the instructions for the new packing procedure?
Yangi qadoqlash tartibi uchun ko'rsatmalarni takrorlay olasizmi?
Supervisor:
Of course. First sort by size, then check for damage, finally pack in groups of 12.
Albatta. Avval o'lcham bo'yicha saralang, keyin shikastlanishni tekshiring, nihoyat 12 tadan guruhlarga qadoqlang.
Supervisor:
There's been a change in your shift pattern. You'll start at 6 AM tomorrow.
Smena tartibingizda o'zgarish bo'ldi. Ertaga ertalab 6 da boshlaysiz.
Worker:
Did I understand correctly? My new start time is 6 AM instead of 7 AM?
Men to'g'ri tushundimmi? Mening yangi boshlanish vaqtim 7 o'rniga 6 mi?
Worker:
How should I report my daily progress to you?
Kunlik muvaffaqiyatim haqida sizga qanday hisobot berishim kerak?
Supervisor:
Send me a text message at the end of each day with the number of boxes completed.
Har kun oxirida bajarilgan qutillar soni bilan menga SMS yuboring.
Supervisor:
We need volunteers for overtime this weekend. Are you available?
Bu dam olish kunlari qo'shimcha ish uchun ko'ngillilar kerak. Sizda vaqt bormi?
Worker:
Yes, I'm available. What time should I come in and what will I be doing?
Ha, menda vaqt bor. Soat nechada kelishim kerak va nima qilaman?
Worker:
I want to make sure I understand the safety protocol for this new equipment.
Bu yangi asbob uchun xavfsizlik protokolini to'g'ri tushunganimga ishonch hosil qilmoqchiman.
Supervisor:
Good question. Always wear gloves, check the emergency stop button, and never operate alone.
Yaxshi savol. Doimo qo'lqop kiying, favqulodda to'xtatish tugmasini tekshiring va hech qachon yolg'iz ishlatmang.
Supervisor:
Your performance has been excellent this week. Keep up the good work!
Bu hafta sizning ish samaradorligingiz a'lo bo'ldi. Yaxshi ishni davom ettiring!
Worker:
Thank you for the feedback. I'll continue to maintain these standards.
Fikr-mulohaza uchun rahmat. Men bu me'yorlarni saqlab qolishni davom ettiraman.
