Grammar / Grammatika

Bu grammatika nima uchun kerak? (Why do we need this grammar?)

Ferma ishida supervayzer va boshqa ishchilar sizga ko'rsatmalar beradi. Bu ko'rsatmalar odatda buyruq shaklida (imperative mood) bo'ladi. Bu grammatikani bilish sizga ish joyida xavfsiz va to'g'ri ishlashga yordam beradi. Shuningdek, siz ham boshqalarga ko'rsatma berishni o'rganasiz.

Asosiy tushuncha (Basic Concept)

Imperative Mood (Buyruq mayli) - bu kimgadir nima qilish kerakligini aytish uchun ishlatiladigan fe'l shakli. Bu juda oddiy: fe'lning asosiy shaklini ishlatamiz. Masalan: "Work!" (Ishla!), "Stop!" (To'xta!), "Come here!" (Bu yerga kel!).

Ferma ishida bu grammatika juda muhim, chunki xavfsizlik qoidalari va ish ko'rsatmalari shu shaklda beriladi.

Amaliy qo'llash (Practical Application)

Ish boshida (At work start):

"Put on your high-vis jacket and safety boots before entering the field."

Dalaga kirishdan oldin ko'rinadigan jilet va xavfsizlik etiklarini kiying.

Xavfsizlik ko'rsatmalari (Safety instructions):

"Never work alone with heavy machinery. Always have someone nearby."

Og'ir mexanizmlar bilan hech qachon yolg'iz ishlamang. Doimo yaqinida kimdir bo'lsin.

Muammo paydo bo'lganda (When problems arise):

"Stop the machine immediately and call for help if you hear strange noises."

G'alati tovushlar eshitsangiz, mexanizmni darhol to'xtating va yordam chaqiring.

Ish jarayonida (During work process):

"Sort the fruit carefully and pack only the best quality ones for export."

Mevalarni ehtiyotkorlik bilan saralang va faqat eng sifatlisini eksport uchun qadoqlang.

Tez-tez qilinadigan xatolar (Common Mistakes)

❌ Xato: "You are wear your gloves" (fe'l qo'shib yuborish)

✅ To'g'ri: "Wear your gloves" (faqat asosiy fe'l)

Buyruqda "are, is, am" ishlatilmaydi

❌ Xato: "Not touch the machinery" (don't ni unutish)

✅ To'g'ri: "Don't touch the machinery"

Inkor uchun doimo "Don't" yoki "Never" kerak

❌ Xato: "Please you check the equipment" (ortiqcha so'z)

✅ To'g'ri: "Please check the equipment"

Buyruqda "you" ishlatilmaydi

Examples / Misollar

Always wear your high-vis jacket and safety boots in the field.
Dalada doimo ko'rinadigan jilet va xavfsizlik etiklarini kiying.
Today we're picking strawberries in greenhouse number 3.
Bugun biz 3-raqamli issiqxonada qulupnay teramiz.
Make sure you sort the fruit by size and quality.
Mevalarni o'lchami va sifati bo'yicha saralashni unutmang.
Report any damaged equipment to your supervisor immediately.
Shikastlangan asbob-uskunalar haqida darhol nazoratchi ga xabar bering.
The irrigation system runs automatically every morning at 6 AM.
Sug'orish tizimi har kuni ertalab soat 6 da avtomatik ishga tushadi.
Your shift starts at 7 AM and finishes at 3 PM with a lunch break.
Sizning smenangiz ertalab 7 da boshlanib, tushlik tanaffusi bilan 15 da tugaydi.
Never operate machinery without proper training and supervision.
Hech qachon to'g'ri o'qitish va nazorat bo'lmagan holda mexanizmlarni ishlatmang.
If the weather conditions are dangerous, work will be suspended.
Agar ob-havo sharoiti xavfli bo'lsa, ish to'xtatiladi.
Pack the vegetables carefully to avoid damage during transport.
Tashish paytida shikastlanmaslik uchun sabzavotlarni ehtiyotkorlik bilan qadoqlang.
First aid kits are located in every greenhouse and field office.
Birinchi yordam to'plamlari har bir issiqxona va dala ofisida joylashgan.

Dialogues / Suhbatlar

Supervisor:
Good morning everyone. Today we're working in field 5, picking carrots.
Hammaga xayrli tong. Bugun biz 5-dalada sabzi teramiz.
Worker:
What equipment do we need for this task?
Bu vazifa uchun qanday asbob-uskunalar kerak?
Supervisor:
Make sure you wear gloves and safety boots. The ground is muddy today.
Qo'lqop va xavfsizlik etiklarini kiyishni unutmang. Bugun yer loyqa.
Worker:
Should we also wear high-vis jackets in the field?
Dalada ko'rinadigan jilet ham kiyishimiz kerakmi?
Worker:
The irrigation system in section B isn't working properly.
B bo'limdagi sug'orish tizimi to'g'ri ishlamayapti.
Supervisor:
Thanks for reporting that. I'll call the maintenance team right away.
Xabar berganingiz uchun rahmat. Men darhol ta'mirlash guruhiga qo'ng'iroq qilaman.
Trainer:
Never operate the tractor without checking the safety features first.
Avval xavfsizlik xususiyatlarini tekshirmasdan traktorni hech qachon ishlatmang.
New Worker:
What should I check before starting the engine?
Dvigatelni ishga tushirishdan oldin nimani tekshirishim kerak?
Worker:
I think I've injured my back lifting these heavy boxes.
Bu og'ir qutilarni ko'tarishda belimni jarohatlagan bo'lsam kerak.
First Aider:
Stop working immediately. Let me check and we'll get you medical help.
Darhol ishlashni to'xtating. Men tekshirib ko'raman va sizga tibbiy yordam beramiz.
Supervisor:
The weather forecast shows heavy rain this afternoon. We'll finish early today.
Ob-havo ma'lumotiga ko'ra bugun tushdan keyin kuchli yomg'ir bo'ladi. Bugun erta tugatamiz.
Worker:
Should we cover the harvested vegetables before we leave?
Ketishdan oldin yig'ilgan sabzavotlarni yopishimiz kerakmi?
Quality Controller:
These tomatoes are too small for export. Put them in the domestic market boxes.
Bu pomidorlar eksport uchun juda kichik. Ularni ichki bozor qutilariga soling.
Packer:
How many kilos should I put in each domestic box?
Har bir ichki bozor qutisiga necha kilo solishim kerak?
Worker:
Can I take my break now? I've been working for 4 hours straight.
Hozir tanaffus qila olamanmi? Men 4 soat davomida to'xtovsiz ishladim.
Supervisor:
Yes, take 15 minutes. Make sure someone covers your section.
Ha, 15 daqiqa dam oling. Kimdir sizning bo'limingizni qoplashini ta'minlang.
Supervisor:
We need volunteers for overtime this weekend. The strawberry season is at its peak.
Bu dam olish kunlari qo'shimcha ish uchun ko'ngillilar kerak. Qulupnay mavsumi eng yuqori cho'qqida.
Worker:
I can work Saturday morning. What time should I be here?
Shanba kuni ertalab ishlay olaman. Qaysi vaqtda bu yerda bo'lishim kerak?
Safety Officer:
Remember, if you see any unsafe conditions, report them immediately.
Esda tuting, agar xavfli sharoitlarni ko'rsangiz, darhol xabar bering.
Worker:
Who should I report safety issues to if you're not available?
Agar siz bo'lmasangiz, xavfsizlik masalalarini kimga xabar berishim kerak?
