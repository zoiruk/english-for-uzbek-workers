Grammar / Grammatika

Bu grammatika nima uchun kerak? (Why do we need this grammar?)

Passive Voice (majhul nisbat) rasmiy hujjatlar va shakllar to'ldirishda juda ko'p ishlatiladi. Bu grammatika sizga rasmiy til va hujjatlardagi ko'rsatmalarni tushunish uchun kerak. Masalan, "Shakl to'ldirilishi kerak", "Ma'lumotlar taqdim etiladi" kabi iboralar doimo passive voice da yoziladi. Britaniyada har qanday rasmiy ish bilan shug'ullanganda bu grammatikani bilish shart.

Asosiy tushuncha (Basic Concept)

Active Voice (faol nisbat): Ish qiluvchi muhim. Masalan: "I fill the form" - Men shaklni to'ldiraman.

Passive Voice (majhul nisbat): Ish muhim, qiluvchi muhim emas. Masalan: "The form is filled" - Shakl to'ldiriladi.

Qachon ishlatiladi:

• Rasmiy hujjatlarda

• Qoidalar va ko'rsatmalarda

• Ish qiluvchi noma'lum yoki muhim emasda

Tuzilishi: be + Past Participle (3rd form)

Uch shakl (Three Forms)

1. Tasdiqlovchi shakl (Positive Form)

Subject am/is/are Past Participle O'zbek tarjima

Amaliy qo'llash (Practical Application)

1. Rasmiy hujjatlarni tushunish (Understanding official documents)

Vaziyat: Shakl to'ldirish ko'rsatmalarini o'qish

Umumiy iboralar:

• "All fields must be completed." - Barcha maydonlar to'ldirilishi shart.

• "Documents should be submitted by post." - Hujjatlar pochta orqali topshirilishi kerak.

• "Applications are processed in order." - Arizalar navbat bo'yicha ko'rib chiqiladi.

• "Forms can be downloaded from the website." - Shakllarni veb-saytdan yuklab olish mumkin.

2. Jarayonlar haqida so'rash (Asking about processes)

Vaziyat: Idoraviy ishlar haqida ma'lumot olish

Foydali so'roqlar:

• "When will my application be processed?" - Mening arizam qachon ko'rib chiqiladi?

• "Is additional documentation required?" - Qo'shimcha hujjatlar talab qilinadimi?

• "How long is the form kept on file?" - Shakl qancha vaqt saqlanadi?

• "Where should the form be submitted?" - Shakl qayerga topshirilishi kerak?

3. Muammolar va tushuntirishlar (Problems and explanations)

Vaziyat: Shakl to'ldirishda muammolar

Muammolarni tushuntirish:

• "The form was not completed correctly." - Shakl to'g'ri to'ldirilmagan.

• "Some information was not provided." - Ba'zi ma'lumotlar berilmagan.

• "The deadline was missed." - Muddat o'tkazib yuborilgan.

Yechimlar:

• "The form needs to be resubmitted." - Shaklni qayta topshirish kerak.

• "Missing information must be added." - Etishmayotgan ma'lumotlar qo'shilishi shart.

Tez-tez qilinadigan xatolar (Common Mistakes)

❌ Xato (Wrong) ➡️ ✅ To'g'ri (Correct)

1. "Be" fe'lini unutish:

❌ "The form completed" ➡️ ✅ "The form is completed"

❌ "Documents required" ➡️ ✅ "Documents are required"

Tushuntirish: Passive voice da "be" fe'li majburiy.

2. Noto'g'ri Past Participle ishlatish:

❌ "The form is complete" ➡️ ✅ "The form is completed"

❌ "Applications are process" ➡️ ✅ "Applications are processed"

Tushuntirish: Passive voice da Past Participle (3rd form) kerak.

3. Active va Passive ni aralashtirib yuborish:

❌ "I am completed the form" ➡️ ✅ "I completed the form" (Active)

❌ "The form completes by me" ➡️ ✅ "The form is completed by me" (Passive)

Tushuntirish: Active da oddiy fe'l, Passive da "be + Past Participle".

4. Modal bilan noto'g'ri ishlatish:

❌ "Forms must completed" ➡️ ✅ "Forms must be completed"

❌ "Documents can submitted" ➡️ ✅ "Documents can be submitted"

Tushuntirish: Modal + be + Past Participle.
